Hotel Description:


The newly-renovated Residence Inn Cincinnati North Sharonville, Ohio Hotel features a new lobby to help you thrive along with great amenities and a convenient location, minutes away from Cincinnati. At our Sharonville, Ohio hotel, you'll have plenty of space to work and relax during your stay. Our 144 comfortable and spacious suites near Springdale feature fully-equipped kitchens, separate living rooms with a sleeper sofa, ample work space, and free high-speed Internet service, allowing you to stay productive. While with us, enjoy a complimentary hearty breakfast buffet and a 24-hour marketplace. During a long day of meetings or events, allow us to take care of errands with complimentary grocery shopping and dry cleaning pick-up services. Socialize during our hospitality hour Monday-Wednesday with courtesy food and beverages. Conveniently located near local restaurants and three major interstates, the Residence Inn Sharonville Ohio Hotel is the perfect Cincinnati-area hotel choice for a longer stay.
