Hotels Close to Basspro in Cincinnati

By Jack Stone

Cincinnati, Ohio, has plenty of attractions to draw in visitors from all over the country and world, from the Paramount's Kings Island theme park to Paul Brown Stadium, home of the Cincinnati Bengals. The Bass Pro Shop in northern Cincinnati, while not a theme park or ball stadium, also attracts visitors to the area who are interested in fishing, hunting or other outdoor recreation. If you are visiting northern Cincinnati and staying near the Bass Pro shop, you can also get in some golfing at the Kingswood Golf Course and the Beckett Ridge Country Club.

Comfort Suites Forest Park

Just across Winton Road from the Bass Pro Shop is Comfort Suites Forest Park, a quality hotel at a reasonable price. Guests will find a flat-screen plasma television, desk, free high-speed wireless Internet, pillow-top mattress, microwave and refrigerator in every room. Some suites include small kitchens and hot tubs. Every morning, the Comfort Suites offers a free continental breakfast and a free copy of "USA Today." Featuring an indoor pool, indoor hot tub and fitness center, this hotel is sure to meet your travel needs.

Comfort Suites Forest Park
1234 Omniplex Drive
Cincinnati, OH 45240
(513) 551-5028
choicehotels.com

Best Western Cincinnati West Inn & Suites

The Best Western Cincinnati West Inn & Suites, located less than a mile south of Bass Pro, has 81 guest rooms and suites. The hotel offers a free continental breakfast every morning, and guests can enjoy the many on-site amenities for free as well, such as the fitness center, indoor pool and hot tub and full-service business center. Every room includes a microwave and refrigerator for any food needs, as well as cable television and high-speed wireless Internet for entertainment. An ideal stay for the guest on a budget, the Best Western near Bass Pro offers adequate lodging for a reasonable price.

Best Western Cincinnati West Inn & Suites
11967 Chase Plaza
Fairfield, OH 45240-1943
(513) 825-9600
bestwestern.com

Extended Stay America Cincinnati--Fairfield

Just under 3 miles from the Bass Pro Shop in Cincinnati is the Extended Stay America Cincinnati--Fairfield. This hotel, ideal for the guest in town for several days or longer, comes with fully equipped kitchens in every room. Guests can expect to find a refrigerator, stove, microwave, dining utensils and basic cookware at their disposal. With several basic grocery stores within a mile of the hotel, preparing meals from the comfort of your hotel room is made easy. Wireless Internet is available throughout the hotel for a one-time fee, but local calls are free. Extended Stay America also includes an outdoor pool and on-site guest laundry.

Extended Stay America Cincinnati--Fairfield
9651 Seward Road
Fairfield, OH
(513) 860-5733
