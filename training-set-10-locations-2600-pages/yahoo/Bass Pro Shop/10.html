Quirky Tourist Attractions in or near Ohio

While in Ohio, you might enjoy a visit to:

Plymouth, Michigan: United Memorial Gardens

A cemetery with quite a bit extra, including a tribute to the Wonders of Michigan and a Gateway to Animal Heaven.
~51 miles from Rossford.

Kenton, Ohio: Gene Autry Mural

Kenton Hardware Company manufactured wildly popular Gene Autry repeating cap guns in the 1930s. The mural depicts the singing cowboy actor waving his hat as his horse rears up in front of the factory.
~66 miles from Rossford.

Sandusky, Ohio: Boy and the Boot Statue

4-ft. tall hollow metal sculpting of a young boy standing, holding aloft a leaky boot that dribbles continuous streams of water. The boy in Sandusky has been leaking since 1874.
~46 miles from Rossford.

Dearborn, Michigan: The Henry Ford, America's Greatest History Attraction

If this place doesn't have it, it probably isn't worth having. Kennedy's death car, Lincoln's death chair, and the last breath of Thomas Edison -- in a test tube!
~51 miles from Rossford.
