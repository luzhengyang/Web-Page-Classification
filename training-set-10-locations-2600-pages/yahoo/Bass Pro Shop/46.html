Easter Egg Hunts & Bunny Sightings in Cincy & NKY 2014

March 25, 2014 By KatieS

Hippity Hoppity..it’s Easter time!  Which means it’s time for Easter Egg Hunts and Pictures with the Easter Bunny!  We’ve put together a list for you of Easter Happenings all over Cincinnati and Northern Kentucky to help you figure out where to have your Easter fun!  If we missed one please leave a comment and let us know!  Hoppy Easter!

Northern Kentucky Egg Hunts & Bunny Sightings ::

 BB Riverboats Easter Cruises ~  Sunday, April 20th, 2014 Brunch: 1 – 3pm Dinner: 5:30 – 7:30pm  Celebrate Easter aboard a beautiful riverboat and enjoy a cruise down the Ohio River while you eat a fantastic meal and hang out with the Easter Bunny!  Visit the BB Riverboats website for all the important details!

 

Benton Family Farm     “Country Easter Down on the Farm” ~ April 12th, 2014 10-2  $10 per carload.  Visit baby animals, hunt for eggs, pictures with the Easter Bunny, hold baby chicks, bunnies and a lamb, crafts and more!

City of Covington Easter Egg Hunt ~ Saturday, April 19 at 1pm at the Volpenhein Pavilion in Devou Park, Free for ages 3-12.  Visit the City of Covington Website for all the details.

Florence Mall ~ Pictures with the Easter Bunny through April 19th.  Visit the Florence Mall website for details about prices, hours, etc.

Fort Thomas ~ Spring Egg Hunt on Saturday, April 19th at noon at Tower Park.  Ages 1-8.  Face painting, bunny pictures, egg hunt and more!

Jane’s Saddlebag in Union, KY is having an Egg Hunt on April 19th at noon.  Hunt for eggs and then grab a bite for lunch at the restaurant.

Kenton County Public Library Events ~

Holiday Happenings: Easter (Covington)  Grades: K – 6 Monday, April 14, 7:00 pm-8:00 pm Join us for Easter stories, crafts and treats.

Easter Eggs! (Erlanger) Saturday, April 19, 2:00 pm-3:00 pm Bring the family for some stories and history of the Easter Egg. Decorate eggs in unusual ways, and learn about other methods you can try at home.

Easter Puppet Show (Erlanger) Ages: 2 – 8  Tuesdays, April 8 & 15, 7:00 pm  Enjoy the antics of the puppets as they prepare for spring and the Easter season. Puppets will entertain by singing, dancing and telling jokes. Make a sticker scene to take home.

Easter Party (Erlanger) Sunday, April 13, 1:30 pm-3 pm   Hop in to visit with the Easter Bunny. Bring your own camera. There will be games and crafts while supplies last.

Pictures with the Easter Bunny (Durr) Ages: 0 – 12 Saturday, April 5, 1:00 pm-3:00 pm  Hop on over to the Durr branch between 1:00 and 3:00 for free pictures with the Easter Bunny and crafts for the kids! Don’t forget to bring your camera!

Kentucky State Parks ~ From Easter Brunches to Egg Hunts, many parks across our state are celebrating Easter, check out the State Parks website for all the details.

 Cincinnati Egg Hunts & Bunny Sightings ::

Anderson Township Park District ~ Easter Egg Hunts for Kids, Teens AND Adults!  Visit the ATPD Website for dates, times, cost, etc.

Bass Pro Shops in Cincinnati will be having Easter Egg Hunts, Pictures with the Easter Bunny, Crafts and other fun stuff going on this month.  Check out the Bass Pro Shop Events Page for more details!

The Cincinnati Zoo’s Easter Celebration ~ April 19 10am until 2pm  There will be treat stations throughout the zoo, activities for the munchkins, animal egg hunts ,visits with the Easter Bunny, and four “Golden Eggs” hidden around the zoo.  Whoever finds the Golden Eggs wins a great prize!

H.J. Benken Florist  Make & Take Easter Egg Wreath Workshop  ~ Make an adorable Easter wreath for your front door!  Saturday, April 5th at 10:30 am.  Children 10 and older must be accompanied by an adult.  Registration required, $20 includes supplies.  Please call 513.891.1040 or email us at classes@benkens.com to register

Kenwood Towne Center ~ Pictures with the Easter Bunny, now through April 19th, visit the Kenwood Towne Center website for details about time, prices, etc.

LM&M Railroad’s Easter Bunny Express ~ Take a ride on the rails to LM&M Junction where you will meet the Easter Bunny himself and hunt for eggs!  Visit the LM&M website for dates, times and to order your tickets.

Milford Christian Church ~ April 11th at 11:00am – come 15 minutes early to register.  Free event up to 5th grade.

Neusole Glassworks: 656 E.McMillan St, Cincinnati, OH 45206    $35 per egg    “Would you like an egg that won’t fade, get eaten, or thrown away? Then come to Neusole Glassworks and make a hot-sculpted Easter Egg today!  Sessions are 30 minutes per person between 10 am – 8 pm  Call (513-751-3292) or email neusoleglassworks@hotmail.com) to schedule

Parky’s Farm Easter Spectacular ~ April 12 & 13: Enjoy lunch at the park, pictures with the Easter Bunny, a hayride and MUCH MORE!  Visit their website for times and ticket information.

TriCounty Baptist Community Easter Egg Hunt    April 12    Beckett Rd West Chester    FREE     Noon – 4pm Food,Games, Bouncy House, Puppet Shows, Photos w/ Easter Bunny, Egg Hunt, and more. http://www.tricountybaptist.org/

Washington Park Easter Egg Hunt    April 19, 2014 | 8:00am – Noon    3CDC    www.washingtonpark.org    Washington Park    FREE    Washington Park’s big Easter Egg Hunt returns Saturday, April 19! The event features 5,000 candy-filled eggs, fun prizes, crafts with Explore Nature and more. The official hunt is open to children between the ages 3 through 12, but participants must bring their own baskets. More information available at www.WashingtonPark.org.

William Howard Taft National Historic site Easter Egg Hunt ~ Sunday April 13, 2014 starting at 2:45 pm.   The Easter egg hunt will be directed by the Easter Bunny. The first egg hunt for children aged 6 and under will start at 2:45 p.m. The second egg hunt at 3:00 p.m. will be for those children aged 7 to 12 years old. All children must be accompanied by an adult and please bring your own baskets and camera.   There will be a magician, games and you can even take a tour of the William Howard Taft National Historic Site.  Admission is Free, Parking is Free.  More information can be found at wwww.nps.gov/wiho, or by calling 513-684-3262.

Winton Woods Golf Course’s Easter Brunch ~ Sunday April 20th visit the Cincinnati Parks website for all the important details like cost, menu, times, and more.

Did we miss your Egg Hunt or Bunny Sighting Event?  Submit your event HERE.

Other Important Easter Happenings:

The St. John Passion Play ~ This play about the death of Jesus Christ  began in 1917 in Over the Rhine, and is in it’s 97th season.  It is performed at St. Augustine Church in Covington and Lockland Christian Church in Cincinnati and runs through April 18th.  Visit the Passion Play website for all of the details.

Fish Fry Dinners : All over town organizations are hosting Fish Fry Dinners to raise money.  Visit our Fish Fry Listings for all the details!
