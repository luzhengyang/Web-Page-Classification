Property Description - Comfort Suites Cincinnati

Brand new hotel located close to the Bass Pro Shop Outdoor World store, several other shopping areas and local attractions.This all suite hotel offers guest suites with microwave, refrigerator, hair dryer, complimentary WIFI, iron and full size ironing board, plasma TV and pull out sofa sleeper in every room. We have an indoor pool, exercise room, business center and guest laundry. We offer a full, hot breakfast each morning and plenty of free onsite parking. Hotel offers Manager's reception every Tuesday at 5 pm.
