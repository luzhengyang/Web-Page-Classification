    Pointers for those who venture forth:

    1. Take Ronald Reagan to the Blue Ash/Kenwood Road exit and enter the mall from the backside (read from the North/West).  There is a lot less traffic from this direction.  This is the side that Galbraith borders.

    2. Park in the garage behind Macy's.  You may actually be able to find a spot, as opposed to the front/Nordstrom side of the mall.

    3.  Go during work hours on weekdays.  Everyone is off after 4 and on weekends, and this is where they go.

    4.  If you have to go on the weekend, go in the morning and go on Sunday.  The wicked rest until about lunch time.
