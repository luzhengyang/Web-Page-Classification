    Why Live Here?
    Welcome to Fontainebleau Milton Apartments. Just minutes after you leave your luxurious Fontainebleau Milton apartment, you can be in the heart of downtown Miami, Coral Gables, Kendall or Miami Lakes. Located just 4 miles from Miami International Airport, Fontainebleau Milton enjoys instant access to all major highways including the Florida Turnpike, I-95, and State Roads 826 and 836. But it's not just business that awaits you nearby...plenty of pleasure also awaits you. Fontainebleau Milton puts you 2 miles of The International Mall, Dadeland Mall, & Florida International University. Therefore, if you want to enjoy close proximity to everything, Fontainebleau Milton is for you. Fontainebleau also offers residents more 5 star amenities such as a newly upgraded 3000 square foot fitness center, including saunas, 2 indoor racquetball courts and year round jacuzzi "Let Fontainebleau Milton put you at the heart of it all". Please call for an appointment today.
