Miami International Airport (MIA): Approximate driving time to the Fontainebleau Miami Beach is 20 minutes and distance is 12 miles.

Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport (FLL): Approximate driving time to the Fontainebleau Miami Beach is 35 minutes and distance is 26.5 miles.

Getting there: Taxi fare to this Miami Beach hotel is $32. All prices are subject to change.

Getting around: Guests of the Fontainebleau can walk or take a taxi to many Miami Beach destinations including the Bal Harbour Shops, Lincoln Road, and South Beach. Taxis are an ideal way of getting to downtown Miami and other nearby destinations. Renting a car is the best means of transportation for those venturing to destinations farther afield, such as the Everglades and other Southern Florida attractions.

Vacation in style

May 5, 2010

Hi there, Big plus: I can highly recommend the place we stayed. We traveled in style. The hotel is big but super nice, sparkling clean, modern and sleek. There are 11 delicious restaurants and about 7 pools. The lobby lounge and club are amazing and definitely worth checking out. Free beach chairs and continental breakfast is also a plus staying at the owner suite. If you are a hotel guest and not renting from the owner they would charge you $20 for a chair and I am sure the breakfast would be around $20 box as well. There is also a great wooden elevated boardwalk you can stroll up and down all the way to Ocean Drive. Great for walks or runs in the later afternoon. Minus: It is about a 7$ cab ride to Lincoln and Collins which is where most of the action happens. So too far to walk but 7 box is not much too. The sand strip on the beach is definitely very narrow and not as nice as further up. You can either walk up or take a cab if you like a wider beach. It depends what you are looking for but it is still nice and quiet. Didn’t see lots of shops around or CVS etc. but might have missed it.
