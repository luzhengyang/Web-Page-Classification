What we like
One thing you will love about the Fontainebleau Miami Beach hotel is its excellent location, which is close to the beach and mall as well as being within easy reach of golfing facilities and cultural attractions. The wide variety of facilities at the resort is also impressive, with plenty of dining options, around the clock room service, entertainment, and some great recreational facilities. There is also free internet access here, so you can stay connected during your stay without having to pay any surcharges for the privilege.

When to get the best rates
If you want to get the best room rates at this hotel you will find that these are generally available earlier on in the week, so you may want to plan your stay accordingly. Rates tend to be higher towards the end of the week and the highest rates apply on Saturdays.

Hotel information
The 4.5 star Fontainebleau Miami Beach is a luxurious hotel that offers a host of facilities for guests to enjoy. There are more than 1500 rooms at this hotel, all of which provide a range of in-room amenities such as flat screen televisions and luxury toiletries. The hotel offers access to a choice of rooms in including deluxe rooms, ocean view rooms, and suites.

You won’t be short of onsite facilities when you stay at this hotel, as there is plenty available for the comfort and convenience of guests. There are eleven restaurants to choose from catering for all sorts of tastes and preferences, a number of bars including one by the pool, a full service spa for those who want a little pampering, a night club, and a number of shops. You also get to benefit from a range of business and conferencing facilities as well as free internet access.

What’s nearby?
Thanks to its great location, you will find yourself near plenty of attractions and places of interest when you stay at the Fontainebleau Miami Beach hotel. Indian Beach Park is just on the doorstep for those who want to enjoy recreational fun and relaxation. If you want retail therapy you will find that Lincoln Road Mall is within each reach. Golfing enthusiasts will be able to head to the nearby Bayshore Municipal Golf Course, which is also close to the hotel.

Wizard’s insider moneysaving tips
• The hotel offers one bedroom suites with kitchens – if you want to avoid spending money on eating out, this could be a great option
• If you want a view of the ocean but don’t mind a side view rather than a full view you can save money by opting for an ocean view room rather than an ocean front one

Location description:

With a stay at Fontainebleau Miami Beach in Miami Beach (Mid Beach), you'll be minutes from Indian Beach Park and close to Lincoln Road Mall. This 4.5-star hotel is within close proximity of Scott Rakow Youth Center and Mount Sinai Medical Center.

Dining info:

Enjoy a bite to eat at a coffee shop/café, or stay in and take advantage of the hotel's 24-hour room service. While enjoying a refreshing dip in the hotel pool, you can order your favorite drink at the poolside bar.

Amenities info:

Pamper yourself with a visit to the spa, which offers massages, body treatments, and facials. You're sure to appreciate the recreational amenities, including a nightclub, a health club, and an outdoor pool. Additional amenities include complimentary wireless Internet access, gift shops/newsstands, and a hair salon.

Business amenities info:

Featured amenities include a business center, limo/town car service, and express check-in. Event facilities at this hotel consist of conference space and meeting rooms.

Room detail info:

Make yourself at home in one of the 1,504 air-conditioned rooms featuring minibars and flat-screen televisions. Complimentary wireless Internet access is available to keep you connected. Bathrooms have complimentary toiletries and hair dryers. Conveniences include safes and desks, and housekeeping is provided daily.
