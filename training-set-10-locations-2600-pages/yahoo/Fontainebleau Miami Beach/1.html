Fontainebleau II - Tresor

4441 Collins Ave, Miami Beach, FL 33140

       

This 36 story all suites tower was built in 2005 on the 22 acres ground of world famouse Fontainebleau Miami Beach. It has 460 units mostly junior suites about 500 square foot and one-bedroom suites a little over 1000 square foot. The Tresor condo-hotel is ideally located in the Heart of Miami Beach, minutes from South Beach and Downtown Miami; perfectly set just far enough away from South Beach for peace and quiet, yet near enough to join the action! It is also a place of choice for President and Vice-President when they visit Miami.

As a guest or owner of Fontainebleau II Tresor, you can choose to use any or all of the resort's social and recreational amenities. Enjoy the pampering, primping, and fitness facilities of a world-class spa - massages, body treatments, facials and skin care sessions, health and exercise classes. Make your stay here a never-ending culinary experience at award-winning indoor and outdoor restaurants. There are also shops to visit, exciting activities programs, and nightly entertainment in the true tradition of the Fontainebleau.

As an owner of the Tresor properties you can enjoy excellent program if you wish to get income while you are away and delegate management of your unit to Fontainebleau hotel.

Fontainebleau Miami Beach Amenities

  • 22 acres of incomparable recreation.
  • 11 fine dining and casual restaurants and lounges, including world class "Gotham Steak" by chef Alfred Portale, "Scarpetta" by Scott Conant and "Hakkasan" featuring award-winning Chinese cuisine.
  • Chic and trendy nightclub "LIV", glamorous sushi bar "Blade", dazzling "Blue Bar" and casual yet sophisticated pool bar "Glow"
  • Shopping arcade.
  • World class Spa and Fitness Center, offering a full program of classes and services.
  • 1,200 feet of white sand oceanfront beach.
  • 6 pools: giant free-form infinity pool; exclusive European-style pool with a sexy enclave surrounded by cabanas and sun loungers; family oriented small dipping pools and an interactive children’s waterscape play area with giant water slide.
  • Poolside restaurant, bars, snack bars and incomparable service.

Fontainebleau II Tresor Features

  • Exclusive Owner's Lounge for continental breakfast, and social activities with TV sets and pool table.
  • Private pool accessible only by Tresor owners and guest.
  • Room service, maid service, laundry/dry cleaning service, valet parking.
  • Central air conditioning, Central heat, Electric heating
  • Junior suites have small electric range microwave oven, refrigerator...
  • One-bedroom suites featured full size kitchen, two full bathrooms, two balconies, walk-in closet.
  • Flooring: carpet with marble in kitchens and bathrooms.
  • Pet friendly
