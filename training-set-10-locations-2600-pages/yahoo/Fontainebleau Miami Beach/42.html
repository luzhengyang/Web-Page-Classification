    Fontainebleau Miami Beach

    4441 Collins Avenue, Mid Beach, Miami Beach, United States

    Show Map

    Overview

    Very Good, 8.4
    Ocean views
    Superb dining
    Stunning pool
    1,433 guest reviews

    Offering stylish rooms complete with luxury linen and coffee makers, this luxurious hotel features a spa & wellness centre and a swimming pool. This property is conveniently located in Mid Beach.

    This Miami Beach hotel boasts free wireless internet access in public areas and a fitness centre. Welcoming and professional team members are available 24-hours a day.

    Featuring luxury toiletries and a private balcony, Fontainebleau Miami Beach's rooms offer 4.5-star accommodation. All are air conditioned and equipped with a seating area, an oversized bathtub and a refrigerator.

    Fontainebleau Hotel Miami Fl houses a restaurant and a bar where travellers are able to relax of an evening.

    Fontainebleau in Miami is surrounded by the area's well-known tourist attractions, and The Forge is a 20-minute stroll away. The hotel's multilingual staff are available to ensure your stay is hassle-free.
