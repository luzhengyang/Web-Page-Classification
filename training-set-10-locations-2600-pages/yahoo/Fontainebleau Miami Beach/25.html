    Fontainebleau Miami Beach

    4.0

    This legendary hotel first opened its doors to the public in 1954, when the A-list celebs of the time - Elvis, Sinatra and the Rat Pack - were frequent visitors. Following a $1 billion renovation and expansion in 2008, the hotel offers 22 oceanfront acres of a spectacular blend of Miami's golden era and stylish modern luxury. There is a sophisticated poolscape with water features including the secluded Oasis Pool and an interactive waterscape play area that will be a hit with the kids. The hotel also has a 40,000 square foot, state-of-the-art health spa and a range of 11 restaurants and nightclubs.

    Where is it?

    On 22 beachfront acres in the heart of mid Miami Beach. Transfer time from Miami airport: 25 min.

    Larger Map

    Getting around

    Valet Parking from $38 per day (excluding tax, payable locally, subject to change) .

    Room information

    1,504 rooms and suites, on up to 37 floors in 2 buildings of the original design, Chateau & Versailles and 2 modern state-of-the-art towers with suites, all 4 buildings have lifts. All have air-conditioning, plasma TV with pay-per-view films and games, iMac computers with free Internet access and iPod stations, WiFi, marble bathroom and safe. Chateau/Versailles Standard Rooms have 1 king-size bed or 2 queen-size beds, sleeping max 4 adults.

    Room Upgrade

    Chateau/Versailles Ocean View Rooms with ocean views have 1 king-size bed or 2 queen-size beds, sleeping max 4 adults. Deluxe Balcony Rooms with a private balcony offering ocean views or views of Biscayne Bay and Downtown Miami have 1 king-size bed or 2 queen-size beds, sleeping max 4 adults. Trésor Junior Suites with a kitchenette, mini-fridge and microwave, sleeping max 4 adults. Trésor 1-Bedroom Suites have a separate master bedroom and sitting area, a balcony with impressive views of the Atlantic Ocean or downtown Miami and a fully-equipped kitchen with fridge, oven and microwave, sleeping max 5 adults. Sorrento Junior Suites offer ocean views and have a kitchenette with mini-fridge and microwave, sleeping max 4 adults. Chateau/Versailles Ocean Front Rooms offer ocean front views with a furnished balcony and have 1 king-size or 2 queen-size beds sleeping max 4 adults.

    Eats and drinks

    11 restaurants and lounges including gourmet Italian, Chinese and a Steakhouse, as well as casual international and French Mediterranean cuisine and the newest Michael Mina 74 offering American Bistro cuisine. Most restaurants feature indoor and outdoor seating areas. Arkadia Lounge, Bleau Bar and Miami’s hottest new night club LIV.

    Fun and games

    Poolscape featuring a total of 6 pools. Lapis Spa offering numerous treatments* and hair and nail salon*, boutique, fitness centre and relaxation areas. Water sports* from the beach including jet-skiing, windsurfing and much more. Special kids' area with cabanas and lounge chairs which allow parents to relax while overseeing their children at play. The resort’s youngest guests can enjoy an interactive play area with a beach-style entry, waterslide, spray canopy, water canons and zero entry wading pool. Kids' programme* (4-12yrs). Golf, tennis and diving available nearby.

    Handy stuff

    Variety of shops, to include 3 upscale clothing boutiques. Laundry services*.

    Additional information

    * Denotes local charge.

    Please note: There may be a local charge for use of a roll-away bed for parties of 5 people or more sharing a room. This is subject to change and is charged by the hotel.

    A resort fee of $21.95 plus tax per room per day is payable locally and includes use of the following amenities: Unlimited wired and wireless Internet in guest rooms and at the pools, in-room iMac computers with internet access, admission to the gym, beach lounge chairs for up to 4 people per room, unlimited local and 800 calls and daily newspaper.

    Accessibility

    Wheelchair accessible rooms/suites have roll-in showers with 1 king-size or 2 double beds.
