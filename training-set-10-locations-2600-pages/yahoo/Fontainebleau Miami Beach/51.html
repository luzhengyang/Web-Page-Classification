Can I change my date at a later date?

We understand that your plans might change upon arrival at your destination, and that you may need to reschedule your tour or travel experience.

That's OK!*

Once your booking is confirmed, you'll print a voucher with complete reservation details, including the tour operator's local telephone number. You can call them directly with any last-minute questions or date-change requests. And they'll look after you, subject to availability of course! Don't forget to mention you hold a confirmed booking voucher, made with us.

* Excludes tickets for shows, musicals, theater and other events that are not for exchange or 100% non-refundable (as per Air Canada Vacations's Terms and Conditions).
