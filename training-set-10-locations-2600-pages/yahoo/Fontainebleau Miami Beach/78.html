Miami Beach oceanfront hotel with full-service spa

On the beach

This famous Miami Beach hotel rises above 1,000 feet of Atlantic Ocean beach, just 2 miles from shopping on Lincoln Road and 3 miles from Miami's South Beach.

Health spa, pools

The Fontainebleau features 6 oceanfront pools surrounded by luxurious private cabanas. Dining and entertainment options include 12 restaurants, bars, cafes, and nightclubs as well as a health spa and personal watercraft rentals.

i-Mac computers

Rooms and suites include premium bedding with 400-thread count Egyptian-cotton linens. All offer wireless Internet access, iMac computers, advanced sound systems with i-Pod docks, and flat-panel TVs.

At your hotel

At your hotel

The Fontainebleau Miami Beach is situated on 22 acres and rises above 1,000 feet (305 meters) of Atlantic Ocean beach. Dining and entertainment options abound with 12 restaurants, cafes, bars, and nightclubs. The 40,000-square-foot (3,700-square-meter) Lapis spa, specializing in thermal therapies and mineral-rich waters, has 30 private treatment rooms, a co-ed jetted pool, and lounge area.

Outside, an extensive pool area includes a large free-form main pool, an exclusive European pool, multiple dipping pools, and an interactive children's waterscape. A kids' camp, offering daily activities, is also available. Private cabanas surround the pools and feature flat-panel TVs, compact refrigerators, and a butler service. Guests can also rent beach cabanas, umbrellas, chairs, and personal watercraft.

Eat and drink

FB Steakhouse - The menu expands beyond modern American steakhouse fare to include fresh seafood, specialty cuts of meat, and more than 500 wines from around the world. Located on the lobby level of the chateau. Open for dinner daily.

Hakkasan By Alan Yau - Offers diners Chinese fare and a diverse menu of new-world wines and sake. The restaurant is located on the fourth floor rooftop that affords guests views of the beach and Atlantic Ocean. Open for lunch and dinner daily.

Scarpetta By Scott Conant - This Italian restaurant highlights Conant's regional menu comprised of clean, crisp flavors prepared in layers to reflect the quality of locally sourced ingredients. Featuring ocean and poolside views as well as casual dining areas and a cocktail lounge. Located on lobby level of Sorrento. Open for dinner daily.

La Cote - A chic, 2-level, poolside restaurant with a relaxed environment. It provides simple, accessible fare to those looking to dine casually and in style. The open-air restaurant on the upper level overlooks the pool and ocean and offers guests the flavorful cuisine of the south of France. Open for breakfast, lunch, and dinner daily.

Vida - Capturing the essence of the glamorous history at Fontainbleau Miami Beach, Vida is an American coffee shop in a contemporary and relaxed environment. The restaurant caters to guests seeking a casual quick meal or a more social dining experience. Serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner, Vida offers an à la carte menu with classic breakfast selections, daily lunch specials, traditional deli sandwiches, and a varied dinner choice from pasta to seafood.

Michael Mina 74 - Created uniquely for Fontainebleau by award-winning chef Michael Mina, this venue delivers a sophisticated American bistro-style menu. Its fun, whimsical dishes are easy to share and perfectly suited to Miami's sultry climate.

Fresh - Offering a wide selection of simple fare to be enjoyed in the restaurant or for take out. Located on the ground level of the Chateau. Open daily.

Chez Bon Bon - Offers exquisite, hand-crafted, one-of-a-kind, edible creations, as well as a selection of custom signature cakes and patisseries to go.

LIV - A 30,000-square-foot (2,800-square-meter) nightclub with DJs, 3 full-service bars, and private skyboxes.

Bleau Bar - The Fontainebleau's signature bar presents late afternoon entertainment along with a full bar and wide selection of excellent wines and classic cocktails by Master Mixologist Michael MacDonnell; serves light bar fare. Open daily from 10 AM until late evening.

Blade Sushi Bar - Japanese fare featuring fresh, daily-caught sushi. Selection of purist sake and a variety of sake cocktails. Opens onto pool area and private cabanas. Opens at 10 AM daily.

Glow Bar - Located in the main pool area. Serves cocktails, snacks, and sandwiches.

Relax

The two-level Lapis Spa harnesses the natural qualities of water to created a shared experience that is both restorative and renewing. Mineral-rich water in many forms - including mist, rain, and steam - are used throughout the 40,000 square foot spa that features 30 private treatment rooms as well as a co-ed jetted pool and lounge area.

Lapis draws upon thermal therapies the oldest spa treatments that transfer energy from heated elements of Earth, Water, and Air. Merging these age old techniques with the latest contemporary technology and design, Lapis provides guests with a stress-relief, anti-aging, and wellness solutions - each a sensory experience designed to energize and rejuvenate.

Spa interiors, treatments and programs are unexpectedly re-interpreted, creating an environment inspiring social engagement that is contemplative, pure, and sophisticated. A communal sanctuary, Lapis provides intimate moments within a quiet collective.
