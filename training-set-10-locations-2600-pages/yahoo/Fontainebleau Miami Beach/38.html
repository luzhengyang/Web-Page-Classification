This beachfront hotel with spectacular ocean views features 6 outdoor swimming pools surrounded by private cabins. It offers 2 nightclubs and a wide variety of dining options.

Guests can relax in the lounge chairs by the pools, or in one of the private cabins including a flat-screen TV, Wi-Fi and a butler service. There is also a children’s playground and direct beach access.

All rooms at Fontainebleau Miami Beach feature carpeted floors and an elegant décor. They include a flat-screen TV with cable channels and an iPod docking station. Some have a balcony with ocean views.

Fontainebleau Miami Beach offers a wide range of cuisines, including Chinese, Italian, and Mediterranean food. There is also a coffee and cake shop.

Miami International Airport is a 20 minute drive away.
