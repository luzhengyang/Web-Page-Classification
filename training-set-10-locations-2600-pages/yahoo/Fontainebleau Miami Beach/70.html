Fontainebleau Resort & Towers Miami Beach

Videos Write a Review Read Reviews Post a Comment Map Event Listings(1) »
Check-in:Check-out:Guests:

First class dining, recreation and nightlife in this four-star, four Diamond resort located directly on the Atlantic Ocean. The Fontainebleau has the distinction of being a scene in numerous films shot in Miami, including Al Pacino's notorious Scarface. Or, you can call hotels.com toll-free 800-695-8284 (U.S.).
