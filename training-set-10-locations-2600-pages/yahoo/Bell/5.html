The Application:

Taco Bell has a well organized career system in place via their website tacobell.com and affiliated links. Through this website you will find the Taco Bell application online and also a wide variety of other information such as job descriptions, available positions, company information and more! Because taco Bell locations are franchised, your job application will best be begun by searching for the location you wish to work. After entering the information for your search area you will notice a new window with the available opportunities and information on that location. Selecting the position you are interested in will take you to a page with the general description and the link to apply. You may also refer someone else to the position. Once you have select the locations you wish to apply to, you will be asked to create a login profile. This will help you when and if you need to login to your saved application to update information, your resume, etc.

The Taco Bell job application takes between 30-40 minutes to complete so be ready to devote that time to the task. You will be asked to verify that you are over 13 years old and that you agree to the terms and conditions of Taco Bell’s parent company Yum Brands. Taco Bell is more than just a great burrito and salsa; they have an assortment of job opportunities to match their bright, festive company. People of all ages, races, and walks of life work at Taco Bell and diversity is a positive attribute to Taco Bell restaurants.  A Taco Bell application is your best first step to reaching the opportunity of a lifetime.

Age Requirements:

Labor laws vary by state in the United States, and may be different depending upon your location. Federal law mandates that teenagers under 14 may not be employed; however those between 14 and 17 may be employed with restrictions. You may be able to work with Taco Bell at the age of 16, however it is always best to check with your preferred location when you submit your Taco Bell application online to be sure that you will qualify.

Application Review and Interview: 

The online Taco Bell application is created in a very simple, step by step manner. You do not have to worry with complicated procedures and protocol as you navigate the websites and information links. Taco Bell application and follow up procedures may follow a similar guideline to those below:

  • For restaurant location applications, your application will most likely be reviewed by the assistant or store manager. The store manager or operator may work independently or collaboratively to review your application. Additionally, decisions regarding employment may also be a collaborative effort. Because Taco Bell restaurants are franchised, you may find that the person interviewing you and handling your application is local to only that restaurant location. Should you have questions, it is best to field them toward that location and not toward a generic customer service hotline.
  • Corporate positions may follow a different procedure using human resource or Yum Brand recruitment procedures. You may still submit an online application for these positions; however the protocol may be slightly different. During the interviewing process there may be more than one interview and interviewer, depending on the position, location, and job situation.
  • To work at Taco Bell, you may be asked to consent to a background and drug test. Make sure that you are prepared to consent to this, and that you can schedule the drug test in a timely manner to avoid delays in beginning employment.
