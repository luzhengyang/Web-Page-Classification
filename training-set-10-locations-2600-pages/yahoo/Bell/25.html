A Northern Oasis


The Skeena Mountain Range and Bell Irving River provide a dramatic backdrop for this unique location. Once a basic service station for travelers on their way to Alaska and the Yukon, Bell 2 Lodge has since been developed into a modern wilderness resort. Now equipped with satellite communication and state-of-the-art environmental facilities, it offers the comforts of a first class lodge with a rustic touch of the Canadian wild.

Whether you are traveling north or south between Vancouver & Alaska/Yukon, Bell 2 Lodge is a must stop. Enjoy the best burger between Smithers and the North Pole or even something for the more delicate palate. You will be greeted with a friendly smile and the best of Canadian hospitality.

Heli-Skiing: In the winter months the lodge is home to Last Frontier Heliskiing. It is considered to be one of the premier heliskiing operations in the world and boasts the largest single heliski area on the planet!
