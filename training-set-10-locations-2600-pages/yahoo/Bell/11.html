Vancouver Neurologists is the area’s chief resource for diagnosing and treating neurological disorders. It was founded in 1980 by Dr. Robert Kim and Dr. J. Bruce Bell, as Southwest Washington’s first Board-certified Neurologist.

We assess the full range of neurological problems and sleep disorders.

We can treat all that we diagnose. We can assume care for disorders with long-term follow-up.

We handle the administrative burden for you. We call patients, schedule them, input insurance information, handle pre-screening and pre-authorization.

EEGs for all ages, beginning with neonatal. All EEGs interpreted by Board-certified neurologist.

MRIs on site. All studies interpreted by Board-certified radiologists. MRIs of the brain interpreted by Neuroradiology experts.

Long-time partner with area physicians and chiropractors.

Staff are highly sensitive to the stress experienced by patients. Practice, open, supportive communication.

We diagnose and treat disorders of the nervous system, including diseases of the brain, spinal cord, nerves, and muscles. Some of the problems we address include stroke, epilepsy, headaches, dementia, Alzheimer’s disease, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson’s disease, and various forms of pain.

Vancouver Neurologists is the industry leader in understanding and using the latest that the specialty has to offer. We are known for innovative approaches, depth of knowledge, and compassionate care.

We assess the full range of neurological problems.

All of our physicians are highly experienced.

We have state-of-the-art digital equipment and ability to store records indefinitely.

Our NCV/EMG Studies are done by physicians rather than by technologists. Often, studies are interpreted by the same physician who treats. We offer quick, expert explanations to our patients and to referring providers.

We accommodate patients quickly; provide results promptly, including preliminary reports issued immediately after exam.
Our physicians network among themselves, giving a built-in “second opinion” to their recommendations.

All of our Electroneurodiagnostic technologists complete a national registry of competency through ABERT (The American Board of Registered Electroneurodiagnostic Technologists). Dr. Dietrich is a Board-certified Electroencephalographer.

Staff is highly sensitive to the stress experienced by patients. We practice, open, supportive communication.

We offer EEGs for all ages, beginning with neonatal.

“Short bore” MRI machine allows patient’s head to be outside the scanner, except for head scans. All studies are interpreted by board-certified Neuroradiologists.

Our Vancouver Sleep Disorders Center is accredited by the American Academy of Sleep Medicine. Medical Director Dr. William S. Herzberg is Board-certified in Sleep Medicine.
Our sleep technologists are registered by BRPT (Board of Registered Polysomnographic Technologists).

We assist with all insurance needs/information, and handle pre-screening and pre-authorization. Categories:   Pediatricians | Neurologists | Sleep Disorders | General Practitioners
