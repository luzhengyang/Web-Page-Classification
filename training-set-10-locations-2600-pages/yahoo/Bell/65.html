SITE OF 2014 NW NATURAL PARADE OF HOMES!

Erickson Farms is a new community located in the heart of Felida, near Vancouver Lake. With a great location and nice territorial views, this neighborhood has so much to offer.

Conveniently located off of NW Lakeshore Avenue at NW 106th Street in Vancouver, Washington, Erickson Farms is the perfect location to build your dream home.

Erickson Farms is being built in three phases with home values ranging from $400,000 to $1 million. We are currently building Phases 1 and 2, consisting of 116 single-family residential building lots ranging from 5,500 - 13,700 square feet. We are now taking reservations for townhomes, ranging from 1,900 - 2,956 square feet.

For more information about available homes, lots, and floor plans, contact us today.
