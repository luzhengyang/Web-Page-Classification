    Located in downtown Vancouver, Washington USA at the corner of 6th and Columbia, the Hilton is convenient to Interstate 5 and just minutes from downtown Portland and the Portland International Airport. The 226-room hotel and Vancouver Convention Center opened on June 15, 2005 and welcome convention groups and business and leisure travelers with all of the full-service amenities expected at a Hilton and the charm of a small urban neighborhood.
  • Custom architecture and interior design make the Hilton Vancouver Washington a unique property. Designed and decorated by a local firm using the majority of materials and artwork from local craftsmen, guests will feel at home with the warm, modern design. Signature touches include the lobby fireplace and grand granite staircase, original artwork and comfortable furniture groupings. The front desk is staffed 24 hours a day to assist our guests, and the concierge is available to assist with restaurant recommendations, directions, transportation, and anything else that would make your stay more enjoyable. Just off the lobby, Esther's Espresso Bar is available for beverages and snacks, as well as our gift shop and business center.

At A Glance

Relax at Hilton Vancouver Washington, a contemporary downtown Vancouver hotel offering central convenience with a pet-friendly and environmentally conscious approach. Situated in the heart of downtown Vancouver, Washington, minutes from the Vancouver Amtrak Station, this downtown Vancouver hotel provides easy access to Portland, Oregon and Portland International Airport. Valet parking and self-parking are available, with complimentary parking available for hybrid vehicle owners.

Located adjacent to Esther Short Park and within easy reach of Portland Expo Center, Clark Country Event Center & Sleep County Amphitheatre, this Vancouver hotel is ideal for events. Hold business conferences and meetings of any size in 30,000 sq. ft. of flexible space with a fully equipped business center. Arrange outdoor conferences and enlist the assistance of the hotel Event Team to rent Esther Short Park. For smaller events, the convenient eEvents booking system allows full event management online.

Reinvigorate in the hotel's indoor lap pool and whirlpool after a workout in the fully equipped fitness center, a complimentary feature of this stylish Vancouver, Washington hotel. Sample contemporary Northwest cuisine in Gray’s at the Park, or recharge with a coffee at Esther’s Coffee Bar located on-site. Spend the day in downtown Vancouver and take a stroll along the nearby promenade that runs parallel to the Columbia River. Spend time browsing the wares in the local boutiques, shops and galleries.

Highlights

  • Central downtown Vancouver hotel, minutes from Vancouver Amtrak Station
  • Excellent location for access to Portland, Oregon and Portland International Airport
  • 30,000 sq. ft. of flexible meeting space
  • Stylish casual, patio and private dining at Vancouver restaurant Gray’s at the Park
  • Contemporary, pet-friendly Vancouver hotel with a fitness center, pool and whirlpool

What to do around here

Downtown Vancouver is the cultural and commercial center of Vancouver, and our hotel is located right in the heart of it. The city offers an array of attractions and entertainment, as well as access to many local and national business offices. The central locale of Hilton Vancouver WA provides an ideal base from which to visit attractions.

Spend time in Esther Short Park, the oldest park in the state of WA, and explore the bell tower, rose garden, Slocum House and impressive fountain system. A farmers' market and children's playground are also in this popular public park. Discover breathtaking vistas at the protected National Scenic Area, Columbia River Gorge, a 4,000-ft. deep canyon that stretches for 80 miles, forming the boundary between the state of Washington and Oregon. Witness the scenes and historical significance of an active volcano, Mount St. Helens. Or, stay in and enjoy dinner in our on-site bar and grill, Grays at the Park, offering fresh and local Northwest cuisine.
