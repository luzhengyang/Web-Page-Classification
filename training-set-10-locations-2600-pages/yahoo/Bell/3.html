Conquer The Mountain!

"Delivering an Uncommon Golf Experience For All"

Green Mountain Golf Course brings the game of golf to you in fun and very affordable ways. Players of all abilities, from Vancouver, Camas, and surrounding areas, enjoy the game here with friends and family.  Whether played from the “tips” or from our unique family tees, you’ll find a well-conditioned golf course that anyone can play and enjoy, regardless of ability.

Located at the southern base of Green Mountain on the Vancouver/Camas border, Green Mountain Golf Course is a short drive for many who enjoy the relaxed, country atmosphere and beautiful surroundings. Getting a tee time is easy – just glance to the left and reserve your tee time online (with no booking fees). Or call our golf shop and one of our friendly staff will assist you.

We guarantee our lowest rates are only available through this website.

Tee time specials are as low as $56 for a foursome.  Hate playing alone? Looking for someone new to play with?  We can help you find a game here. 


Find out why over 90% of our customers would recommend Green Mountain to their friends and family, and why Green Mountain Golf Course should be the location of your next round of golf. We strive to consistently exceed your expectations and are very fond of advising our customers: 

"Don't play too much golf. Two rounds a day are plenty..."   -- Harry Vardon, six-time British Open Champion

See you on the first tee!
