Dinner

Monday - Sunday
5pm - 10pm

Our menu changes with the season and honours the bounty of the Pacific Northwest on land and sea. Start with selections from our varied raw bar including Albacore tuna, freshly shucked oysters, and seasonal ceviche; then move on to entrées crafted around western British Columbia specialties such as Haida Gwaii halibut and westcoast inspired paella.
