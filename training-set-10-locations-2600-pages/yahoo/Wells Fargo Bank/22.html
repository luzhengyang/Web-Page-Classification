Current Rates for Ordering Foreign Currency Online

Print this page
 

The current foreign exchange rates for ordering foreign currency banknotes (cash) online are shown below. Before placing your online order, you may use the convenient Currency Calculator tool to determine how much foreign currency banknotes will cost at the rate shown below.

The rates for ordering foreign currency banknotes in the store or on the phone are similar to the rates below but may not be the same.

All rates for ordering foreign currency banknotes online are quoted in U.S. dollar terms, meaning that one unit of foreign currency multiplied by the rate equals the U.S. dollar cost per unit of foreign currency. In addition to the cost of the currency, shipping and handling fees may apply to online orders.

For inquiries on any other rates, such as the rates to exchange foreign currency banknotes back into U.S. dollars, please contact us at 1-800-626-9430.
