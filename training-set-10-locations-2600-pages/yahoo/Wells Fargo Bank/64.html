Tickets

2013 Ticket Details

The 2013 annual Championship Ticket Renewal Offer will begin October 1st and conclude October 31st

All 2012 Championship ticket purchasers will be contacted by email to renew their tickets for the 2013 event. Please note, the Ticket Renewal offer will only be sent by email (not US Mail).

This is a simple two-step process:

August — Update or confirm your contact information when you receive our email

October — Respond by ordering your tickets during the offer timeframe of October 1-31

Be sure to adjust your spam filters to allow emails from wfcgolf@listwriter.com.

All patrons should receive this advance offer to purchase tickets prior to our public ticket launch that begins in mid-November. If you don’t receive your renewal information October 1, please contact the tournament office at 704-554-8101, so we can research the issue and extend to you the offer.

If you have not purchased tickets for the tournament and would like to be added to our ticket information email distribution, please fill out the 2013 ticket information form.
