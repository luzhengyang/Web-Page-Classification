Loan Processor
Posted this month in Virginia Beach, VA

"I feel that the company is so large that they seem to have difficulty truly appreciating the value of their employees."

What do you like about working at Wells Fargo Bank?

"I like the combined efforts between me, the loan officer, and the settlement agent in making clients happy. I enjoy working with clients and building a relationship with them. It is a great feeling when I am able to put a client at ease about the loan process."

Do you have any tips for others interviewing with this company?

"I think that they should ask to be walked around and given an opportunity to meet their potential peers. They should ask questions and look around at the work area to determine if it's the right environment for them."

What don't you like about working at Wells Fargo Bank?

"I feel they demand a lot of time and energy from their employees but do not necessarily give a lot."

What suggestions do you have for management?

"I feel that my direct management does the best that they can, considering the current market and circumstances. With the recent merger between Wells Fargo and Wachovia, I feel the company is just experiencing growing pains, and it may take a couple of years for things to settle down."
