NorthCross Family Physicians is located off Interstate 77, exit 25, in the heart of Huntersville, NC. Our practice provides comprehensive medical care to the residents of Huntersville and surrounding areas.

Services provided at NorthCross Family Physicians include:

  • Health maintenance
  • Adult healthcare exams
  • Pediatric carewell visits, newborn care, sick/acute care
  • Gynecologic services for women of all ages including pap smears, breast exams and contraceptive care
  • Geriatric care

View a full list of all our services

Our board-certified physicians and entire team of compassionate medical professionals are dedicated to providing the highest quality care and excellent service to our patients.
