DIAGNOdent Finds Hidden Decay Early

DIAGNOdent is a small laser instrument that scans your teeth for hidden decay that can develop beneath the hard surface enamel of your teeth. Early detection of problems minimizes the expenses related to major tooth repair.

Learn more…

Waterlase Dental Laser Technology

Waterlase combines laser energy and a spray of water to perform a wide range of dental procedures on teeth, gum and bone more comfortably. Typically, Waterlase dentists use fewer shots and less anesthesia reducing patient anxiety and providing a more relaxing dental experience.
