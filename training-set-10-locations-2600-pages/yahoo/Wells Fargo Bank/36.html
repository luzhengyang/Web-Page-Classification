Aloft Charlotte Ballantyne
Area

Zipping into Charlotte’s stylish southern sector on a business trip or carefree vacation? Aloft Charlotte Ballantyne offers easy breezy access to the entire area—but is light-years from all those other run-of-the-mill hotels. Abuzz with the energy of discovery and interaction, this bold new lodging alternative will surely inspire you to explore our namesake neighborhood and its scenic surroundings.

Stay and play awhile! You’ll discover a lovely locale in the heart of the upscale lifestyle community of Ballantyne, full of chic shopping, delectable dining, and country-club comforts. We’re perched along the grassy greens of the Golf Club at Ballantyne Resort, yet just steps from the booming businesses of the Ballantyne Corporate Park. Venture a bit further afield to discover outdoor excitement at the U.S. National Whitewater Center or family fun at Carowinds theme park.

For a change of scenery, dash into downtown Charlotte in just about 20 minutes. Do business in the bustling Uptown district—or escape the office for sports action and other urban adventures!

Need local tips? Check out Aloft Charlotte Ballantyne’s in-touch kiosks in our re:mix(SM) lounge, for easy breezy Internet access to all that need-to-know info. Then ease on down the road and experience our corner of North Carolina.
