    CHARLOTTE, N.C. Federal officials released surveillance photos of a man who robbed a bank near UNC Charlotte Tuesday afternoon.

    The FBI said a white man with a thin build robbed the Wells Fargo bank located at 8700 JM Keynes Drive in northeast Charlotte. The bank is located near the intersection of North Tryon Street and West W.T. Harris Boulevard just west of UNC Charlotte.

    The FBI says the man robbed the bank around 4 p.m. Tuesday and is approximately 5 3 to 5 6 .

    If you have any information about this incident you are asked to call Crime Stoppers at 704-334-1600 or the FBI at 704-672-6100.
