    Apartments that are Just Right!! A great Harris Blvd Location, near Wells Fargo/Wachovia Campus, UNCC and Carolina Medical Center

    Harris Pond Apartments in Charlotte, North Carolina, offers our residents exquisite apartment homes in a prime location. Our community is only minutes from I-77 and I-85. Residents can find shopping and dining just blocks away at Northlake Mall and the University of North Carolina at Charlotte is right around the corner. We offer 1 and 2 bedroom garden-style apartments. All of our apartments feature screened-in patios and connections for full sized washers and dryers; some homes include a washer and dryer. Select homes have professionally remodeled gourmet kitchens that include stainless refrigerators, a digital stove and microwave and gorgeous ceramic tile floors. We are proud to offer a resort-style swimming pool and 24-hour fitness center. Our walking trail circles a beautiful pond where you could spend an afternoon fishing at your leisure. A 24-hour business center is also available. Visit us today to reserve your new home at Harris Pond!
