                        Riot Fest Food Vendors Include Wow Bao, Dimo's, Wishbone

                        By Kyla Gardner on September 12, 2013 2:45pm | Updated on September 12, 2013 4:08pm
                        Follow

                        @gardnerkyla

                        View Full Caption
                        DNAinfo

                        HUMBOLDT PARK — The food vendors for this weekend's music fest and carnival Riot Fest features Chubby Weiners, Dimo's Pizza, Wishbone Restaurant and Wow Bao, according to the fest's Facebook page.

                        The fest also announced the beer selection, which includes Greyskull Wonder Beer, brewed by Cobra Lounge, Old Style, PBR, Dos Equis, Dos Equis Amber, Magic Hat No. 9, Magic Hat Seance, Strongbow Cider and Sutter Home Chardonnay.

                        The three-day festival takes place Friday through Sunday at Humboldt Park and features headliners The Replacements, The Pixies, Blink 182 and Fall Out Boy.

                        Fest-goers will not be able to bring their own food or drinks but both will be available for purchase inside, including vegetarian and vegan options.

                        "There are beers in every price range from PBR to craft," said a spokesperson for the fest.

                        Single-day tickets were still available for each day on the fest's website.
