gotta love the soul food

i’ve been to wishbone a few times for brunch, and i have yet to be disappointed. the portions are enormous, the menu is electic, and the food is delicious! the atmosphere is on the loud side, making for a boisterous start to anyone’s morning. i’d gladly go back to the wishbone, despite the often long wait during the weekend rush and spotty service. (i hear oprah likes to eat here too!)
