About this Location

W Washington Blvd & N Morgan St
Chicago
IL
60607

W Washington Blvd & N Morgan St has a Walk Score of 85 out of 100. This location is Very Walkable so most errands can be accomplished on foot.

W Washington Blvd & N Morgan St is a four minute walk from the Green Line and the Pink Line at the Morgan stop.

This location is in the Near West Side neighborhood in Chicago. Nearby parks include Trio Apartments, West Wayman Street, Chicago, IL, Skinner Park and Fulton River Park.

Nearby schools include Merit School of Music, Chicago Virtual Elementary Charter Schl and Coyne College.

The closest grocery stores are J.P. Graziano Grocery, N & G Produce and choice eating.

Nearby coffee shops include La Colombe Torrefaction, Starbucks and Dunkin' Donuts. There are 198 restaurants within a 15 minute walk including Wishbone Restaurant, Crazy Moon Espresso Bar & Deli and Flat Top Grill.

W Washington Blvd & N Morgan St is near Mary Bartelme Park, Lexington College and Heritage Green Park.

There are some bike lanes and the terrain is flat as a pancake. W Washington Blvd & N Morgan St is very bikeable, biking is convenient for most trips.
