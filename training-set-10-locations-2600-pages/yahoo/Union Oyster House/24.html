Union Oyster House - Ye Old Oyster House

 


Americas oldest restaurant, established in 1826, is located on the Freedom Trail near Faneuil Hall. Specializing in hearty portions of Yankee-style seafood, lobster and shore dinners, served in a lively, quaint historic setting. Children's menu, full bar, private dining room, reservations recommended.

41 Union Street Boston, Ma 02108
Phone: 617-277-2750 Fax: 617-227-6401 P, AE, DC, DIS, MC

Open Sunday-Thursday 11:30am-10pm; Friday-Saturday 11am-10pm. Union Bar open til midnight. smoking in bar only.
