About this job

Job description

To sell FedEx Canada’s Freight services to both ‘new’ and ‘existing’ customers with the key deliverable of achieving assigned revenue objectives (as measured and determined by FedEx) and to sell FedEx Express and Ground services to ‘existing’ FedEx Freight customers with the key deliverable of enabling multi-OpCo revenue growth.   

Targets new business opportunities either by phone, email, or face-to-face, and schedules appointments with the ultimate objective of acquiring profitable new business in the transborder LTL market and is responsible for the maintenance and/or revenue growth for all FXF accounts in the assigned district with focus on growth and Business Plan attainment. Maintains regular and consistent communication with other FedEx Canada Sales Professionals for joint sales calls, lead sharing, mentoring, etc. Maintains knowledge/awareness of market including competition, customs, pricing, product classification.

 

Leverages relationships with existing Freight customer base to sell full FXE/FXG suite of products and services with specific objective to grow XOpCo revenue.

 

Develops, prepares and delivers formal presentations that are tailored to meet our potential/existing customers’ needs. Prepares and presents proposals (from single location to complex multi-location organizations) while utilizing/adhering to FedEx Canada’s pricing guidelines to maximize revenue and yield, while selling FedEx Canada’s portfolio of products. These proposals may include other FedEx OpCos.

 

 

 

Desired Skills and Experience

 

  • Bachelors Degree or equivalent
  •  2 years business to business sales experience 
  • Thorough knowledge and understanding of the CAN/US LTL market, including pricing and competition preferred
  •  Basic level of proficiency in MS Office: Powerpoint, Word, and Excel 
  •  Strong sales ability and a high degree of professionalism required
  •  Strong oral and written communication skills
  •  Strong presentation, relationship, and negotiation skills
  • Overnight travel required (5-10%)
  •  Valid Class G driver’s license preferred

 (Note: For all job submissions you will be required to attach/create a resume)
