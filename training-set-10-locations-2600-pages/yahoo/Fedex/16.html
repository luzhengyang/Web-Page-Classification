FedEx® International Express Freight (IXF)

International Express Freight is a fast, reliable airport-to-airport solution for shipments of virtually all commodities, sizes and weights.

Service Details

Delivery Times Airport-to-airport delivery, transit times vary depending on origin, destination and availability (typically 2 or 3 business days).*
Service Days Monday to Friday, with Saturday pickup in Toronto and Montreal.
Delivery Area Major world markets [Countries Served]
Skid Size and Weight
  • Skids exceeding 70" (178 cm) in height, 119" (302 cm) in length and/or 80" (203 cm) in width require prior approval.
  • Skids exceeding 2,200 lbs. (997 kg) require prior approval.
  • Individual pieces exceeding 150 lbs. (68 kg) must be palletized, stackable, forkliftable, shrink-wrapped and banded to a skid.
  • No minimum per-shipment or per-piece weight.
  • Unlimited total shipment weight.
Additional Information
  • Advance confirmation required.
  • Drop-Off Locations
  • Airport-to-Airport delivery with FedEx® International Broker Select option only.
  • View Terms and Conditions
Value-Added Service Options
  • Saturday Service (only where Saturday is a regular business day)
  • Dangerous Goods
Special Instructions
  • To book space, call the FedEx Freight Solutions Team at 1.866.744.7493 Monday to Friday between 8:30 a.m. and 8 p.m. EST.
  • Complete the FedEx IATA (023) Air Waybill and fax a copy to 1.866.744.7255 or email to: fxdocs@mail.fedex.com.
  • To help reduce shipment delays and damage, single pieces of cargo greater than 150 lbs. (68 kg) must be banded on all sides with heavy-duty metal or break-resistant plastic banding, or encased in a hardened container (e.g., plywood or metal) and secured with an exterior lock or clamp. Please note that containers secured with glue, nails or screws without an exterior lock or clamp are not acceptable.
