Driving directions to community
Take I-285 to Lawrenceville Highway. Travel northeast to Montreal Road, to the first street on right. Turn right and go approximately 1.1 miles. Avalon on Montreal will be on your right.

 


Northlake Apartments Clarkston, GA

When you are choosing your apartment home, are you selecting not only an apartment, but also a neighborhood. Our Avalon on Montreal apartments are surrounded by shops, restaurants, and parks and boast fantastic connectivity to Northlake, Decatur, and Downtown Atlanta.

  • Avalon on Montreal is in Clarkston, Georgia whose slogan is "Small town, BIG Heart"
  • There is a new Aquatic Facility being planned for Milam Park
  • Friendship Forest is a 15 acre parcel designated by the City of Clarkston as Public Green space
  • There is a plan for 3.8M worth of streetscape improvement projects to be performed by the GDOT
  • Montreal road offers superior connectivity with access to I-285, I-85, 78 and Lawrenceville highway
  • We are on the bus line with a bus stop right at our entrance
  • We are minutes away from great shopping in the Northlake Mall area and of course Decatur!

Points of Interest - Milam Park, Milam Park Dog Park, Milam Park Tennis Courts, Milam Pool, Friendship Forest
