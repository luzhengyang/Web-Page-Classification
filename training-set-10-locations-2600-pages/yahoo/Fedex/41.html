Cheap FedEx Field Hotels

Home of the Washington Redskins, FedEx Field is the largest stadium in the National Football League. It is located in Landover, Maryland, and was originally named the Jack Kent Cooke Stadium until the naming rights were sold to Federal Express in 1999.

Football fans will appreciate the grandeur of the stadium, which boasts three tiers of yellow and red seats that circle the playing field. Behind both end zones, there are video boards that help fans stay immersed in all the action of the game. A large number of luxury suites are available for those who wish to watch the game in style. FedEx Field also boasts several restaurants that overlook the field.

An impressive amount of Redskins history is on display at FedEx Field, which has a Redskins Ring of Fame that circles the seating bowl. It contains 40 names of significant Redskins players and coaches, and is a must-see for any fans of the team. Championship flags that commemorate the team's playoff history hang proudly above the north end zone.

By Rob Bruce
