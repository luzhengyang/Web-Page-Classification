Pickup or Drop-off

Whether you choose the convenience of scheduling a FedEx® pickup or drop off your packages, we make it easy.

FedEx Drop-off Service

You can drop off your FedEx package or parcel at any of our over 1,000 convenient neighbourhood locations across Canada.

To find your nearest FedEx drop-off location and hours of operation, use the FedEx Locator

FedEx Staffed Locations

FedEx World Service Centers
At FedEx World Service Centers, you can drop off your FedEx Express and FedEx Ground packages, pick up FedEx shipping supplies and talk with our knowledgeable customer service representatives if you need assistance. For FedEx Ground package drop-off, you must either use a pre-printed FedEx Ground shipping label, or you can pay cash for your shipment. Credit cards are currently not accepted for FedEx Ground shipments.

FedEx World Service Centers are owned and operated by FedEx and are conveniently located in metropolitan business districts, airports and busy thoroughfares. Find a World Service Center now

FedEx Office
At FedEx Office locations, you can drop off your FedEx Express and FedEx Ground packages. FedEx Office staff can help you choose the service that's right for you and help you prepare your shipping documentation. Find a FedEx Office location now

 

Self-Service Locations

FedEx Drop Boxes
Drop off your FedEx Express packages, pick up shipping materials and get shipping instructions at any one of hundreds of Drop Boxes in office buildings, malls and street corners across Canada. Find a drop box now

 
 
FedEx Retail Affiliate Locations
Staples®
Staples® is among the retailers with in-store FedEx Express drop-off facilities and packaging supplies. Staples locations also provide FedEx Express self-service drop boxes, instructions and shipping materials.		  
 
FedEx Authorized ShipCentre® Locations
FedEx Authorized ShipCentres provide access to FedEx® services across Canada. You can drop off FedEx shipments, purchase FedEx services, and receive personalized packaging assistance at these locations. FedEx Authorized ShipCentres are located at pack and ship stores that provide other business related services.

Find a FedEx Authorized ShipCentre now

Find out how to become a FedEx Authorized ShipCentre®  
 
PostNet Canada®
FedEx delivery services are available at PostNet Print and Business Centers, for your packages going across the country or around the world.		  
 
Pak Mail Canada™
Pak Mail Canada is the specialty shipping company that has what you need to package and ship anything anywhere in the world.		  
 
Envoy
Ship with FedEx at your local Envoy store - A Resource for the Home Based Business Community.
