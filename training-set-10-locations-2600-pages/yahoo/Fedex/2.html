About Us

With over 30 years experience in the transport industry we can handle any facet of your transportation needs. We offer trucking services, several levels of domestic and international air express and air freight services, same day USA and Canada services, special project shipments and when you have more time we can offer sea freight containers or special ocean freight needs. With any shipment we offer as much speed as you need.

Competitive rates are achieved by maintaining solid working relationships with service partners worldwide, and by making efficiency everyone's business.

At Aeromax you can be assured of unsurpassed service when you need it - during or after the business day - without the tightly regimented schedules the overnight air express companies require. All this and more from a company that provides service to some of the most demanding customers in the world.
