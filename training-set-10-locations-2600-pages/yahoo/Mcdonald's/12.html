our friends at McDonald's

 
 
 

Long time, committed friends, McDonald's Restaurants of Canada Limited, McDonald's franchisees, as well as Ronald McDonald House Charities of Canada, are a vital fundraising support for Ronald McDonald House Toronto. In-kind and cash support raised through events, suppliers, coin boxes and McHappy Day are an invaluable resource. The House is incredibly grateful for this ongoing support.
