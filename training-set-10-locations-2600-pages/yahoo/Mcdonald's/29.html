McDonald's $1.39 Value Picks Menu

  • Posted March 7, 2011 @ 9:07am
Get This Deal

McDonald's $1.39 Value Picks Menu is the lowest priced of the major fast food chains but also has some of the best variety thanks to both breakfast and after breakfast items. In the morning you can get the following for $1.39 each:
  • Muffin and Coffee
  • Sausage McMuffin
  • Sausage Buttermilk Biscuit
After breakfast it's over to their all-day Value Picks Menu which includes the following:
  • Junior Chicken
  • McDouble
  • Bacon Cheeseburger
  • Small Fries
  • 2 Apple Pies
  • Coffee and Muffin
  • Vanilla Cone
  • Small Soft Drink
The Value Picks Menu should be available at all stores though the pricing may vary depending on location.
