At A Glance

This Markham hotel near Toronto is the only AAA-Four Diamond all-suite hotel in York Region and is conveniently situated in the Greater Toronto Area. Only 25 minutes from downtown Toronto and Toronto Pearson International Airport (YYZ), Hilton Toronto/Markham Suites Conference Centre & Spa is near the attractions of central Toronto and beyond.

Featuring 26 meeting rooms totaling 45,000 sq. ft. of function space, this Toronto hotel plays host to briefings in chic boardrooms and meetings for up to 1,200 on the doorstep of Toronto corporations such as IBM, American Express, Johnson & Johnson and Motorola.

Our two-room suites are a home away from home. Get to work at the desk or over coffee in Absolute Atrium Lounge with WiFi in all public areas. Sofa beds and two 32-inch LCD TVs make suites a great option for families. Accessible suites are designed for comfort and ease of movement.

Discover fine standards of dining at this Markham hotel. The Sunday market brunch buffet at Essence of Unionville is a delicious hotel tradition, or enjoy afternoon tea buffet on the weekend.

Indulge at eforea: spa at Hilton with luxurious treatments that leave you feeling rejuvenated and reinvigorated. Choose one of three customized experiences created to provide balance and wellness for both body and soul. Keep fit at Club Markham. Squash courts, an extensive weight room and a heated, indoor pool align with a full complement of aerobic and fitness classes.

What to do around here

Discover Central Counties, with downtown Toronto less than 25 minutes from Hilton Toronto/Markham Suites Conference Centre & Spa. Take in breathtaking views from the world's highest observation deck - 1,467 ft. up in the CN Tower. The Henry Moore sculpture collection is one of the many highlights at Frank Gehry's Art Gallery of Ontario.

For the thrill seeker, Canada's Wonderland is only 15 miles away. Rollercoasters including the flagship 77mph 'Behemoth' await. More leisurely tourist attractions can be found at nearby Historic Main Street Unionville. Shop, browse and enjoy Main Street, virtually unchanged since 1794. Be part of the fun at Main Street Unionville events at Millennium Bandstand, such as the Markham Jazz Festival. Two PGA championship golf courses at Angus Glen are only three miles from Hilton Toronto/Markham Suites Conference Centre & Spa.
