Domino's Pizza of London, Ontario was founded in 1984. The store, on Wharncliffe Road, was the second Domino's store in all of Canada. Today, we have six stores and two seasonal stores in London which are part of the global team of over 9, 000 stores. Although Domino's Pizza is an international company, the stores in London remain locally owned and operated. The Domino's team in London is proud of the strong ties with the community and is grateful for the opportunity to serve the community for 27 years.
