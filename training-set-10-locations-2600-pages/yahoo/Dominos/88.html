    Fire Prevention Week: Barrie

    Ian Maclennan
    October 02, 2014 11:15 am

     

    Barrie Fire and Emergency Service has many activities planned to help promote this year’s theme ‘Smoke Alarms Save Lives: Test Yours Every Month.’ A working smoke alarm is the single most important device that you can have in your home to prevent fire tragedy, and can be the difference between life and death.

    Barrie residents are encouraged to participate in the following events:
    Home Depot Fire Safety Day, 10 Barrieview Drive
    Oct. 4, 10am – Noon
    Bring the kids out for this fun event. A fire truck will be on display outside of Home Depot and Barrie Firefighters will work with kids on a fire safety craft inside the store. Participants are required to register on site as space is limited. The event is in partnership with the Fire Marshal’s Public Safety Council.
    Fire Safety Day at Lowe’s, 71 Bryne Drive
    Oct. 4, Noon – 2:00pm
    For another great event on this day, join Barrie Fire as they begin Fire Prevention Week with a full scale fire safety day at Lowes. There will be a free BBQ, fire truck and the bouncy fire truck.

    Fire Station #1 Open House, 155 Dunlop Street West
    Oct. 5, 10:00am – 2:00pm
    Chief John Lynn invites you to tour Barrie’s downtown Fire Station. See the fire trucks and meet firefighters who will be on hand to answer your questions. Activities include: the bouncy fire truck, mini-combat challenge, face painting, movies and popcorn.  This event is FREE!

    New Sunnidale Playground Opening & Official Fire Prevention Week Kick-Off
    Oct. 6, 10:00am (near Dorian Parker Centre)
    Join Barrie Fire as they officially kick-off Fire Prevention Week and open a new fire-themed playground in Sunnidale Park! ‘Every Kid’s a Hero Adventure Playground’ is a fully accessible park with new and exciting features like a modified training obstacle course, toy fire trucks, as well as a fire rescue zip-line!
    11th Annual Home Escape Night & Street Party
    Oct. 8, 6:30pm
    Win the ‘Home Escape Night & Street Party’ by voting for your ward in our social media contest! Barrie Fire will select a street in the ward that receives the most votes, and show up with a street party complete with Domino’s pizza and family-friendly activities!  Voting closes at 9:00am on Wednesday, October 1st EDT. Visitwww.facebook.com/cityofbarrie to select your ward and cast your vote!
    Domino’s Pizza Partnership
    Oct. 9, 6:00pm – 8:00pm
    Dinner is on us – if you have a working smoke alarm! Once again, Barrie Fire has partnered up with Domino’s Pizza to deliver pizza and to check if residents have functioning smoke alarms on every level of their home. If the smoke alarms are present and functioning, the pizza is free!
