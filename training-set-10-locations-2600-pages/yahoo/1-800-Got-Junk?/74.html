About 1-800-GOT-JUNK? Toronto

1-800-GOT-JUNK? is a full-service junk removal company operating our junk hauling franchise in Toronto and G.T.A. since 1999. We'll remove almost any junk, trash, or waste from old furniture and appliances to garden waste, electronics, and renovation debris. We do all the loading and cleanup, wherever the junk is.

1-800-GOT-JUNK? removes household or commercial junk in Toronto and the GTA including Mississauga, Scarborough, Don Mills, Markham, Unionville, Etobicoke, Richmond Hill, Thornhill, Brampton, Ajax, Downsview, York Region, East York, North York, Oshawa, Pickering, Vaughan, Willowdale, Bolton.

We make every effort possible to recycle or donate furniture, household items, scrap metal and all kinds of different junk that we remove from homes and businesses. We work with Toronto charities and recycling centers in the GTA to reuse and recycle the junk we haul away before we take items to the landfill.
