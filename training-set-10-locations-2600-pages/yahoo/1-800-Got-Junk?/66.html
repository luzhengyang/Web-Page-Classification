Toronto Home Show

Yesterday I had a chance to go to the Fall Home Show with our newest team member Lorne, who many of you should meet soon, and Nancy, our founder.  I got a chance to talk in person to some our “HomeStars” – companies listed on our site – as well as meeting some new companies.

I really enjoyed talking to Gary from TrimWork by Design. He told us that HomeStars has been great for his company and has got lots of business from our site. Gary had the great idea of getting him some HomeStars stickers for his trucks reminding his customers to write reviews. Don’t worry Gary, we’re on it and we’ll have them to you soon!

Also Brian Harris from Toronto Clear View Windows was a pleasure to talk to. We’ve exchanged some emails in the past but it was good to put a face to the name.

The folks Cadillac Custom Shutters had a great booth, espousing the quality they obviously have from their great reviews.

The fine ladies from Consumer Choice Home Improvements seemed to be drumming up some good business from the show.

The enthusiasm and energy of the guys from 1-800-Got-Junk was encouraging. I told them about a little pile of concrete I have in my back yard I need removed, and they promised to come personally and get rid of it.

There were many more people I met at the show and I have a few more pictures which I will try and post later.

Looking forward to the International Home Show in a few weeks.
