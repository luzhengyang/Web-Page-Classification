Markham

Home > Our Ronald McDonald Family Rooms > Markham

The first of its kind to open in York Region, the Ronald McDonald House Toronto Family Room at Markham Stouffville Hospital was completed in April 2013.

The Family Room provides families with an infant or child in the Paediatric Unit or the Neo-Natal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) with a much-needed place to rest and recharge while being only steps from their child’s hospital bedside.

Located in 951 square feet of the hospital’s new wing, outside the NICU and near the Paediatric Unit, the Ronald McDonald House Toronto Family Room offers families a space to have a snack, watch TV, read a book and take a shower.

The Ronald McDonald House Toronto Family Room is open from 9am to 9pm, seven days a week.
There is also a napping area available 24 hours a day.
