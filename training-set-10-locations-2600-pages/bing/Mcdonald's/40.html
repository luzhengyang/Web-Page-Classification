(4.7)

For a dining and play experience unlike any other in the world, consider a visit to the World's Largest Entertainment McDonalds & PlayPlace!  

This monstrous, two-story McDonald's restaurant features a play area that boasts 25,000 feet of twisting, turning tubes to play in, as well as slides, games, and other fun for kids of all ages. A 60-foot super slide is a favorite among the more daring children, and more than 60 arcade games and a prize redemption center is a favorite with older kids!

Mom, Dad, and all of the other adults in the group can also find something special. This incredible McDonald's includes the Bistro Gourmet at McDonald's area that offers unique cuisine ranging from pasta dishes to gourmet sandwiches and desserts. You can also find travel and visitors information at the World's Largest McDonald's!
