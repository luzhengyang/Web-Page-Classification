Man Shot Near Dufferin St. McDonalds

April 11th, 2010 9:10am

Manny Rodrigues Photo / ©2010 TSB

NORTH YORK – A young man was shot in the back on Saturday around 5:30pm near the busy area of Dufferin St. and Lawrence Ave. W.

Police say the shooting occurred in front of 51 Blossomfield Rd., just one block east of Dufferin St. where one man was shot. He then fled the scene to a nearby McDonalds parking lot where he pleaded for help.

He was rushed to Sunnybrook Health Sciences Centre with serious but non-life threatening injuries.

Police search the area for up to four suspects. No arrests were made.

A few vehicles, including the victim’s, were riddled with bullet holes.

Local residents were shocked that something like this would happen on a Saturday afternoon when so many people are out and about, including children.

This was the second shooting in Toronto within one hour. Earlier a man was shot and injured on Dundas St. W. in the downtown area. He is expected to recover.
