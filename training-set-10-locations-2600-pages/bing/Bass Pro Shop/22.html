Directions to Bass Pro Shops Atlanta (Lawrenceville): From I-85 Northbound take the Sugarloaf Parkway exit (108). At the Sugarloaf Parkway traffic signal, take a left and go to the next traffic signal. Turn to the right and immediately get into the second lane from the left. This leads into the Discover Mills Mall entrance and directly to Bass Pro Shops.

From I-85 Southbound take exit 109. This exit services two roads. Pass by the first one (Old Peachtree Road) and exit on Sugarloaf Parkway. Turn left on Sugarloaf Parkway and pass under the interstate. Turn to the right at the second traffic signal and immediately get into the second lane from the left. This leads into the Discover Mills Mall entrance and directly to Bass Pro Shops.
