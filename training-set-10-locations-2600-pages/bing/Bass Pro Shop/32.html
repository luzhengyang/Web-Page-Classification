Bass Pro Shops, Dania is located at 200 Gulf Stream Way.

To get to Bass Pro Shops from I-95, take the Griffin Rd exit (exit # 23), go west to the 1st traffic light (Anglers Ave). Make a left on Anglers Ave, another left at the next light (Gulf Stream Way). This will lead you to Bass Pro Shops.

To get to Bass Pro Shops, Dania from the Florida Turnpike, take the Griffin Rd exit heading east to Anglers Ave. Make a right on Anglers Ave. At the next light (Gulf Stream Way) make a left. This will lead you to Bass Pro Shops.

To get to Bass Pro Shops, Dania from State Rd. 7/highway 441, exit on Griffin Rd heading east to Anglers Ave. Make a right on Anglers Ave, left at the next light (Gulf Stream Way). This will lead you to Bass Pro Shops.

To get to Bass Pro Shops, Dania from I-75 go east, connecting to 595, take 595 East to I-95 south, take the Griffin Rd exit (exit # 23), make a right on Griffin (heading west). Make a left at the 1st traffic light (Anglers Ave.) Make another left at the next light (Gulf Stream Way). This will lead you to Bass Pro Shops.
