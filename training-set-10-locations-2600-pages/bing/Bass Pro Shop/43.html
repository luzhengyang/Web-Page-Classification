    Regular Maintenance prolongs the life of your boat!

    Limited time offer:

    Free outside boat storage with purchase of winterization and shrink wrap service.  Offer does not include battery storage and periodic charging.

    Basic Winterization: change gear lube, grease fittings, corrosion guard, stabilize fuel, Anti-freeze (I/O), unhook or remove batteries, remove drainplug, and ensure all drains are clear of debris.

    This limited offer is available until the storage yard is full.

    Call Tracker Marine Boat Center (419) 891-3266 for more details and to schedule an appointment.
