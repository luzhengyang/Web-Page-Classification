Directions to Our Store:

In Cincinnati-- We are located off I-275 on exit #39, which is the Winton Rd./Gilmore exit toward Fairfield. Located between the Mt. Healthy/127 exit and Fairfield/Springdale Route 4.

From Dayton-- Take I-75 South to exit #16 (I-275 West). Go 5 miles to exit #39 (Winton Rd./Gilmore). Take the first right off the exit ramp.

From Columbus-- Take I-71 South to exit #17B (I0-275 West). Go 10 miles to exit #39 (Winton Rd./Gilmore). Take the first right off the exit ramp. From the South (Lexington/Louisville)-- Take I-75 north through Cincinnati to exit #16 (I-275 West). Go 5 miles to exit #39 (Winton Rd./Gilmore). Take the first right off the exit ramp.

From Lawrenceburg, Indiana-- Take I-275 North/East to exit #39 (Winton Rd./Gilmore). Take the first right off the exit ramp.

Accomodations: The Amerisuites by Hyatt is located less than 1 mile from the store at 12001 Chase Plaza Dr. Forest park, OH 45240. When making a reservation, ask for the special Bass Pro Shops rate.

Other nearby hotels include: -Hampton Inn at 430 Kolb Dr. Fairfield, OH (513) 942-3440 -Best Western at 11967 Chase Plaza Forest Park, OH (513) 825-9600
