Quirky Tourist Attractions in or near Ohio

While in Ohio, you might enjoy a visit to:

Fairfield, Ohio: Jungle Jim's International Market

Playful brainchild of store owner Jim Bonaminio, the elaborate, animated displays distract children long enough for parents to complete shopping.
~13 miles from Cincinnati.

Petersburg, Kentucky: Answers In Genesis Creation Museum

Lavish attraction devoted to explaining the difference between Man's Reason and God's Word. Dinosaurs, scripture, science and morality, stirred vigorously.
~18 miles from Cincinnati.

Brookville, Ohio: Andy D-Day and the 2-Headed Calf

In the basement of a local museum is the head of the ultimate freak bull, Andy D-Day, and his sidekick the 2-headed calf.
~47 miles from Cincinnati.
