1. Re: Bass Pro Shop
Jul 05, 2010, 8:38 AM

The Bass Pro Shop is located in Cincinnati Mall (or old Cincinnati Mills, or whatever they're calling it these days) off of Exit 39 on I-275. Since the Cincinnati area is largely defined by the network of highways and the I-275 loop, it would be accurate to say that Bass Pro Shops is located on the north side of the city. The mall also has a Kohl's, Burlington Coat Factory, Babies R Us, and a variety of stores within and outside the mall.

As far as restaurants are concerned, there is an IHOP and an Old Spaghetti Factory nearby. If you're looking for a wider selection of shops and restaurants, the Tri-County area (which has the much larger Tri-County mall and restaurants like BJ's, Chili's, Dave and Busters, Benihana, just to name a few) is just two exits further on I-275 East from the Cincinnati Mall/Fairfield area.

Cincinnati Maill website: http://www.cincinnati-mall.com/

Tri-County Mall website: http://www.tricountymall.com/
