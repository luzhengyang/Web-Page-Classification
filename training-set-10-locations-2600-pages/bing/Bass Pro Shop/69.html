Bass Pro Shops moving from Forest Fair to West Chester

10/21/13 at 11:48am by Shauna Steigerwald 0 Comments
Tweet

Bass Pro Shops, located at Forest Fair Village (formerly known as Cincinnati Mall) since 2000, is planning a move to a new store at Interstate 75 and Union Center Boulevard in West Chester.

Cindi Andrews writes:

The outdoor retailer’s new Union Centre Boulevard location will be 150,000 square feet, a bit larger than the current store, the company announced Friday. It will include an Uncle Buck’s Fishbowl and Grill, a casual restaurant with a fishbowl-themed bowling alley.

“Our new store will enable us to better serve the region’s many sportsmen and women,” said Johnny Morris, founder of Bass Pro Shops.

Construction will start in 2014, and the new Bass Pro Shops will open in 2015.

Shoppers check out the fish tank at Bass Pro Shops. Enquirer file.
