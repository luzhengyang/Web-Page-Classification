Montréal-Mirabel International Airport

Supporting a yearly average of over 32,000 aircraft movements, Montréal-Mirabel International Airport (YMX) serves the Quebec community of Maribel. Owned by Transport Canada and operated by Aéroports de Montréal, YMX services mainly cargo flights.

FedEx Express, Cargojet Airways, Kelowna Flightcraft Air Charter, and UPS Airlines are among the airlines YMX regularly services. In addition, YMX’s runways have been used for drag racing and other motorsports by the NASCAR Canadian Tire Series.

Address: 12300 Rue Service A 4, Mirabel, Quebec J7N 1E8  |  Phone: (514) 394-7377
