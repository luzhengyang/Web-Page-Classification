Things To Do

Enjoy numerous things to do during your stay at our Hilton Garden Inn Montreal Airport hotel. Sports enthusiasts can catch a game at the Bell Centre, home to the National Hockey League's Montreal Canadiens ice hockey team or visit the Olympic Stadium. Enjoy a day out for the whole family at Montreal Botanical Gardens or visit Montreal Biodome to explore fascinating replicas of four ecosystems found throughout America. No trip to Montreal is complete without a visit to Old Montreal. Discover the picturesque cobblestoned streets, lined with buildings dating back to the 17th Century, and explore museums, stylish French eateries and the riverfront Old Port. In the evening experience the thrills of Casino de Montreal, one of the largest casinos in Canada.
