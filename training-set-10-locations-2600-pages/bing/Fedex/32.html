CART FEDEX 2002 CHAMPIONSHIP - VIDEO RACE RECAP - August 25 2002 -- Molson Indy Montréal -- Circuit Gilles Villeneuve -- Montréal, Québec, Canada. For the first time, Cart did a stop at circuit Gilles Villeneuve in Montréal. Before green Tracy was ready to do his job... he was in fire. At the start, he did what he promised to and hit Tagliani. Bad luck for Brack who was an innocent victim. This was an attrition race. The first half of the race was hard on local favourites Carpentier and Tagliani. Both experienced problems. Carpentier had serious engine problem that end his day. Tagliani lost low gears (probably third gear). Junqueira has a surprise in the pits! Near the end, Fernandez hit the wall at an unusual place before 'Pont de la Concorde'. Franchitti has a solid weekend and he deserved P1. Underdogs: da Matta, Kanaan, Tracy. Mechanical: Dominguez, Servia, Carpentier, Takagi, Junqueira, Dixon. Contact: Brack, Fernandez. This summary stands 04 minutes 54 seconds. Music: Jaws Theme, Coldplay - White Shadows
