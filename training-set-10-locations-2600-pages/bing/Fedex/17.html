Montreal Customs Broker

As Montreal’s most value-minded customs broker, Bay City Brokers is dedicated to delivering great customer service and low clearance rates to all of our clients. Compare our brokerage fees with competitors like UPS and FedEx and you’ll see that our prices can’t be beat!

Whether you are shipping or receiving commercial or retail, agricultural or other commerce products Bay City can help provide insights on ever-changing Health Canada regulations and help you determine if there are any taxes or duties you need to pay before hand. You never have to worry about working with an inexperienced junior employee who is learning the ropes. You always work directly with a licensed Montreal customs broker capable of doing more than just paperwork.

Whether you are using a customs broker service for the first time or a high volume importer Bay City Brokers has the resources, work experience, and reliability to expedite your customs clearance quickly and efficiently.

We are here to help facilitate the customs broker process in an easy, stress-free manner. We are committed to building long term relationships, and helping our clients reduce cost and delays associated with customs clearance.

If you would like to learn more visit the personal shipping page or commercial customs shipping page. Or, if you’re ready to get started contact us at any one of our locations.
