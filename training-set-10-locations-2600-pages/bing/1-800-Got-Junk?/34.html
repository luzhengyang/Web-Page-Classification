About this Role
The 1-800-GOT-JUNK? Sales Center in Toronto is seeking a well-qualified Workforce Management (WFM) Analyst to join our team and help us continue to deliver an exceptional experience to our customers through precise management of our call volume and employee schedules.
Reporting remotely to the Sales Center Operations Manager, this role will be responsible for supporting our world class Sales Centre by reporting the performance of our Agents and their teams to Management on an Hourly, Daily, Weekly and Monthly Basis. The WFM Analyst will manage relief periods of all agents in both the Vancouver and Toronto call center locations to produce the most effective results to meet our high service level commitments. Use of automated and computerized resources and tools to increase effectiveness and efficiency of queue systems will be a major component of the role. Experience working with Aspect systems would be considered a significant asset along with Total View, Blue Pumpkin, Impact 360 or other WFM tools. This position works in conjunction with all other business areas in the Sales Centre to ensure that needs are being met with regards to staffing requirements.
Duties and Responsibilities
• Regular, collaborative and consistent communication with Sales Center Leadership on the status of the Sales Centre
• Provide leadership and queue support for all Agents
• Responsible for building and maintaining reporting capabilities - Frontline operations reporting for senior leadership
• Proper scheduling of Agents based on call arrival patterns
• Responsible for continual process improvements, both internal to the WFM team and to the Sales Center as a whole
• Responsible for ensuring daily adherence and compliance with the schedule as well as daily break management
• Maintain a log of employee absenteeism/punctuality (schedule deviations) on a daily basis; advise supervisory/management staff of noticeable employee absenteeism trends
• Responsible for daily agent timecard processing and addressing payroll inquiries in a timely matter
• Knowledgeable and capable of following Business Continuity Plan (BCP) procedures
• Represents Sales Center interests in cross functional/team meetings, most notably marketing, sales and cultural initiatives respectively
Personal Attributes
• Advanced knowledge of excel is required
• Must have 2+ years of Call Center experience
• Strong attention to detail and highly analytical
• Excellent communication, interpersonal and problem solving skills
• Positive and outgoing attitude
• French Bilingual an asset
• Experience with leadership/directing staff with a respectful but authoritative style
• Willingness and ability to work any time of the day, any day of the week as needed. This role requires consistent weekend shifts and significant flexibility.
