Specialties

1-800-GOT-JUNK? is a full-service junk removal company that helps people regain the space in their homes and businesses. Professional crews remove items from wherever they arelocated, including furniture, clothes and toys, renovation debris, and electronics. Wherever possible items are recycled or donated to charity, with current landfill diversion rates at 61.3 percent.

History

Established in 1989.

Now the world's largest junk removal company, 1-800-GOT-JUNK? has 180 franchises in Canada, the United States, and Australia.
