    Current Employee - Operations Manager

    I have been working at 1-800-GOT-JUNK? full-time (more than 3 years)

    Pros

    Great corporate structure plus great small business training! You really learn how to go out and pound the pavement. Strong sales structure and small business structure. Very autonomous management level.. young company with big goals so you really learn how to set big goals for yourself. Proven systems that as long as you follow you will succeed.

    Cons

    There is a ceiling for earnings if you are the franchise level. You will max out and move on. But it is great for starting out college grads.... Territories are small but focus is the key. Trucks are smaller than some so you are going to landfill often. Recycling can be difficult at times but mandatory.

    Advice to ManagementAdvice

    Open up franchise opportunities or partnerships with younger management... Have better control systems in the trucks... Allow for more room in pricing model or find a way to complete an express service to lower pricing or less windows. However very good environment and great culture.

    Cons

    They way overcharge for their service. They have a guy who travels the country teaching franchises how to overprice while giving the client a smile and some conversation. They call this selling techniques.
