    Detail for Wishbone : Restaurant, American / Cajun/Soul




Wishbone serves Southern-style American cuisine such as creamy shrimp fricassee over rice and delicious sides like corn muffins and Key lime pie. Prices usually range from $4.50 - $12.50. This place serves up a tasty brunch as well--be prepared to wait on the weekends. Call for hours. - sys, 08/17/2002

Southern comfort food goes upscale in this eatery. Menu items include chicken-fried steak, blackened catfish, jalapeno-cheese corn bread muffins, and collard greens. Known for outstanding breakfast, items include eggs Florentine, grits, omelets, and seafood cakes. Open for breakfast and lunch Mon-Fri; Sat-Sun brunch; dinner served Tues-Sun. - Alotta, 03/02/2004

Every time I'm here in the area, I've always noticed a good size crowd there dining... It must be a good place to eat out at! - SR, 12/08/2001
