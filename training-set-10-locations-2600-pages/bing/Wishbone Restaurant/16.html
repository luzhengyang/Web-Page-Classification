West Loop

Downtown Chicago to the United Center, we've got it covered.

1001 W. Washington Blvd. 312.850.2663 map


Located one block from Oprah Winfrey's Harpo Studios and perfectly centered between City Hall and the United Center, Wishbone West Loop was an early participant in the emergence of the neighborhood from a commercial area to one of the city's culinary hot spots. We've been there through it all, quietly serving our own unique brand of Southern Cooking.
