Review by: hsi439 on 6/13/2009
Overall Rating:

Coffee Refill Rating:

Expense Rating:

I love this place! Being from the south, I have yet to find a restaurant that has good grits, until eating here. Their Louisiana style omelet and cheese grits are to die for! I have been to both locations and loved both.

Their ambiance is great!

I highly recommend this place.

Award Nomination: Best Breakfast Ever

Review #17189
Review by: shorty711 on 8/14/2007
Overall Rating:

Coffee Refill Rating:

Expense Rating:

I was introduced to Wishbone a year ago after running a race with friends. Since then I have taken many others to this trendy breakfast spot, and all have agreed that Wishbone has some of the best breakfast in the city. The crunchy french toast is addicting! Other favorites include the hot chocolate, omlettes and chicken apple gouda sausage. Thier corn bread and potatoes are also some of the best breakfast sides I have ever had!! Oh and the decorations on the walls make for great conversation :)

Award Nomination: Best Breakfast Ever
