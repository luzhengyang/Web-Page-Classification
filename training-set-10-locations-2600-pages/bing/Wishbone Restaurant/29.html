Grab Breakfast at Wishbone Restaurant in Chicago

by Presidential Towers on September 26, 2012September 26, 2012

Families generally arise early each morning and either rush off to school or work without having time to enjoy a breakfast together. However, things are a little more relaxed on the weekend and families living in the state of Illinois often go to a Chicago restaurant for breakfast on the weekend. Make it a family tradition to enjoy a big breakfast with your family near your two bedroom Chicago apartment at Wishbone Restaurant.

One of their favorite places to visit is the Wishbone Restaurant, which is located at 1001 W Washington Blvd. They are open seven days a week and serve up Southern, Traditional American and Soul Food. Several different variations of omelettes, pancakes, grits, biscuits and gravy, oatmeal, crayfish cakes, home fries, and black beans are just a few of items on their breakfast menu.

Prices vary with each selection. Reviews about the Wishbone Restaurant  have mentioned that although the service can be slow at times, the quality and the taste of the food, makes up for the slow service. Generally speaking, this Chicago restaurant can be very busy during their breakfast hours. If you are planning to visit the Wishbone Restaurant for breakfast, make sure to arrive their early in the morning.
