    • Wishbone

      Posted by Joyce on 08/19/2013

      Great Food

      Went here for the 1st time last Friday (just moved into the neighborhood). Their food is the best I've had so far! Had fried green tomatoes for appetizer, light and delicious, better than mine! The blackened catfish was perfectly seasoned and prepared. Also, the sweet potatoes were creamy and flavorful. Spinach was also perfect! Service was ok and prices very reasonable. The only price I thought that was a little off was the appetizer, expensive for 3 tiny tomatoes!

    • Wishbone

      Posted by Ex-Customer on 11/20/2012

      Disappointed!

      In the past, I was always happy with whatever I ordered from this location. Tonight, the food was not good. I think the cook forgot about salt and pepper. The macaroni and cheese had NO flavor - imagine that! The collard greens included huge stalks, like the whole leaves were just put into a pot instaed of being chopped. Again, no flavor. The crab cakes were just okay. The only thing I enjoyed was the corn bread muffins.

      No Southern Hospitality here

When we got our food, it was hot but dried out and no fork? had to go get it, staff is indifferent and bored. the place looks fun, but none to be had. hard to believe in such a foodie town that they got it all so wrong. went elsewhere for soup afterwards and good hibiscus gingerale, should have started out there instead
