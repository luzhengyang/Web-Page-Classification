Location About

Wishbone is located one block from Oprah Winfrey's Harpo Studios and perfectly centered between City Hall and the United Center.
Established in 1990, Wishbone West Loop was an early participant in the emergence of the neighborhood from a commercial area to one of the city's culinary hot spots.
We are a family business for going on 24 years .We serve our unique southern style cooking for breakfast, lunch, dinner and brunch and have a large catering department.
