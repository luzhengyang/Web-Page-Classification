Wishbone North in the heart of Chicago's Lakeview neighborhood has been serving up the best in Southern cooking to urban Yankees for 15 years. From comfort food to vegan entrees and Cajun/Creole to Piedmont BBQ, we are known for using the freshest, healthiest  ingredients (no lard!) to prepare, smoke and bake almost everything right here on Lincoln Avenue.

Please try to leave room for dessert. Of course ,you can always take slice of our home-made cobblers, pies or red velvet cake to go, too. In fact, special order a whole pie today!

You can sit cozy at the counter or join friends and family at one of our spacious tables or booths for a fine meal. Our adults-only dining room near the bar—"The Bourbon Room"—is the perfect for those looking for a quieter spot. Be sure to check out the murals by Matthew Owens in the "The Twilight Alcove." Amazing.

We look forward to seeing you soon.

Order Wishbone to go! We can have your Southern repast ready for pick-up (don't forget to order extra corn muffins...). Or we can deliver it to your door. Call 773-549-2663.
Our delivery zone stretches from Kedzie to the Lake and from Bryn Mawr to Fullerton. There is a $4 charge for orders under $200. For order over $200, the charge is 8% of the net total.
Let's celebrate together! Whatever your occasion—a birthday party, a wedding dinner, a breakfast meeting or an office holiday shindig, we can make your event that much more special.

PARTY HERE! 
Our "Bourbon Room" can accommodate up to 55 guests. The bar counter is ideal for buffets. We can also serve pre-planned individual plated meals or create a special menu just for your occasion. 

Our two-top tables can be easily reconfigured to handle larger groups. And the "Twilight Alcove"  is truly something special.

For gatherings of up to 200 people, the full restaurant may be rented out as scheduling allows.

PARTY ANYWHERE! 
We love to take our food show on the road! Breakfast meetings go so much better with a Wishbone spread on the conference table. And lunch—did someone say lunch? Shrimp and grits, pulled pork sandwiches, hoppin' john...Are you hungry yet? From appetizers to full dinners, we will help you create the perfect event.
