The incredible reputation of Miami Beach's Fontainebleau properties has created the opportunity for Fontainebleau III Ocean Club, a condominium-hotel adjacent to the wildly popular Fontainebleau II and the world-famous Fontainebleau Hilton. Together, the three will occupy more than 1,200 feet of beautiful, oceanfront land. No hotel in the area comes close to having that much beach frontage!

Fully decorated and fully furnished, your clothes and personal belongings are all you'll need to check in at Fontainebleau III Miami Beach. Pack for the time you expect to be here

Fontainebleau III Rental Management Program:



Policies

  • There will be no restrictions on your occupancy. You may use your unit whenever you'd like and for as long as you'd like, subject to availability. Your unit, however, is intended for transient residential occupancy and cannot be a homestead/permanent residence.
  • You are eligible for the Hilton's Rental Management Program, as long as you purchase your unit furnished in accordance with the approved furniture package, and provided you enter into a one-year, perpetually renewable agreement/contract with the Hilton, which can be cancelled with 60 days notification.
  • You are not required to sign up for the Hilton's Rental Management Program, if you elect not to rent, or wish to rent your unit through another broker or real estate management company.
  • Hilton, the hotel operator, will set the daily room rental rate.
  • As a point of reference, the current standard room rack rates at the Fontainebleau Hilton are $400/night for off-season and $600/night for prime season. For concierge-class suites, average rate is $600/night off-season and $1,000/night prime season. Rental rates at Fontainebleau III are expected to be higher.
  • Children under 18 free when occupying same room.

Fees

  • Monthly Maintenance Fee includes: electric, water, basic cable, concierge services, valet parking [one car per unit], membership to the future new Health Spa, owners' lounge access, and all grounds services and recreational amenities.
  • You will be responsible for the upkeep costs of your unit, and the Hilton will coordinate that upkeep.
  • Taxes are approximately 2% of the purchase price.

Revenue Splits

  • Revenue from each unit will be distributed from the daily room rates as follows: 10% off the top toward fees and commissions; 50/50 split of the remaining balance between you and the hotel operator, with 3% of your portion set aside for reserves and replacements.
