Fontainebleau Miami Beach, FL

Hotel Details and Family Reviews

4441 Collins Avenue
Miami Beach, FL 33140

Fontainebleau Miami Beach, Exterior

The Fontainebleau Miami Beach is a 4.5 star luxury hotel located in the city of Miami Beach, Florida, and is a high rise building with 37 floors. The nearest airport is Miami Intl. Airport (Airport Code: MIA), a distance of about 8.4 miles from the hotel. This hotel has a total of 1504 guest rooms. Check in time is at 4 PM and check out time is 11 AM. Call ahead in case there are any changes, or if you need late check-out.

There are several family restaurants close to the hotel, including Forge Restaurant & Bar, Chicken Kitchen and Bolero Bar & Grill. Select the above Restaurants tab for a more complete list of Miami Beach restaurants in the area and their distances from Fontainebleau Miami Beach.

Guests may wish to visit nearby Miami Beach Golf Courses, Beaches, Museums, Arenas and/or Botanical Gardens within a short drive from this hotel, including the Bayshore Municipal Golf Course Par 3, Miami Beach and Bass Museum. Select the Tots and Travel Attractions tab above for a more complete list of nearby Miami Beach attractions and their distances from Fontainebleau Miami Beach.

Family Features

Families will benefit from a pool at the hotel especially made for kids.

Other family features: Babysitting Services, Refrigerators are available, an economical option for most families and Stroller Accessible.

Hotel Room Features Include: Coffee Tea Maker, Laundry service, Bath / Hot Tub, Wake Up Service, Express Check-In/Out, Modem / Data Port, Broadband Internet, Shower, Voice Mail, Mini Bar, Air Conditioning, TV, Cable / Satellite TV, In Room Movies, Direct dial phone, Microwave, Safe-Deposit Box, Refrigerator, Clothing Iron, Family Room, Hair Dryer and Kitchenette

Hotel Amenities Include: Disabled Access, Sauna, 24 Hour Reception, Bar / Lounge, Elevator / Lift, Restaurant, Concierge, Parking, Swimming Pool, Security Guard, Banquet Facilities, Massage / Beauty Center and Valet Parking

Business Features Include: Wireless broadband Internet access for guests with WIFI capable laptops and Meeting Rooms

Weather & Climate
When visiting in the Spring and Fall, one can expect average daytime temperatures of about 81 degrees, and nighttime lows of 73. Daytime temperatures in the Summer average 87 degrees, while Winter daytime temperatures are about 74. We recommend checking the Miami Beach, FL weather forecast and information to determine proper attire before travelling on your family vacation.
