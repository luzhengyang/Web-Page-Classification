Fontainebleau Miami Beach

Miami Beach, Florida
Located on Collins Avenue overlooking the Atlantic Ocean, Fontainebleau Miami Beach is a spectacular blend of Miami’s glamorous golden era and stylish state-of-the-art luxury. 

A landmark since its debut in 1954, Fontainebleau Miami Beach has a long history as a dynamic destination resort.  It recently underwent a $1 billion renovation and was transformed by a team of renowned architects and designers to become a spectacular five-star quality property.

 

 

The Fontainebleau Miami Beach consists of three hotel and condo-hotel towers situated on 22 oceanfront acres in the heart of Miami Beach.  It has 1,504 guest rooms and suites, including 846 rooms and suites in the original Fontainebleau buildings and 658 junior, one- and two-bedroom suites in two new all-suite towers.

 

This resort, however, is far more than your typical hotel.  The Fontainebleau Miami Beach features 11 fine dining and casual restaurants, including five signature restaurants and lounges overseen by award-winning chefs. 

 

 

It also has a two-story, 40,000 square foot spa; an expansive poolscape that boasts “walls of water” intermingled with a free-form pool; and modern conference and event facilities. 

 

A multitude of services and amenities awaits owners and guests of the Fontainebleau Miami Beach. 

  • 1,200 feet of white sand oceanfront beach
  • All sorts of water sports, from jet skiing to parasailing
  • Fontainebleau Marina
  • Lighted tennis courts, tennis pro and pro-shop
  • Fitness center
  • Exclusive owners' lounge
  • Fully-staffed lobby, 24-hour security and concierge
  • Over 200,000 sq. ft. of meeting space
  • Express business center
  • Multi-lingual staff
  • Room service
  • Free internet access

Residences are delivered fully furnished.  Rooms and suites are fully equipped with plasma screen TVs, iMac® advanced sound systems and ultra-luxurious amenities.  Modern, sophisticated interior design creates an atmosphere of quiet and calm for effortless relaxation. 

 

The Fontainebleau Miami Beach does not have living restrictions so owners may reside in their condo-hotel residence full time if they wish.  Owners also have an opportunity to place their condo in a professionally-managed rental program. 

 

 

The Fontainebleau Resort is just 3.7 miles from the Miami Beach Convention Center and 3 miles from South Beach.  It is conveniently located near both Miami and Fort Lauderdale International Airports.

 

Prices for the condo-hotel residences range from $289,000 to $9,800,000 for a 4,500 square foot five-bedroom.  Because the development is open and operational, closings can take place within 30 days.
