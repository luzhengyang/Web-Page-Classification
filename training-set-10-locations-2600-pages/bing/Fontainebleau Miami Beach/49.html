Restaurants in the Fontainebleau Miami Beach

By Richard Ludwig, eHow Contributor

The Fontainebleau, a symbol of South Beach, offers some of the finest dining options available in all of South Florida. Each of the hotel's three signature restaurants has a list of dining awards, while the other three eateries offer casual options that are sure to satisfy any palate. From early-morning breakfast to late-night dining, the Fontainebleau Miami Resort has the restaurant to cover it.

  1. Gotham Steak

    • Named for the trendy Gotham Bar and Grill in Manhattan, Gotham Steak combines the temperance and refinement of a New York steakhouse with the flair of South Beach cuisine. The dual-level restaurant, with indoor and outdoor seating, offers a dozen steak options from Kansas City bone-in to the classic New York strip. The menu also features seafood, with one of the tastiest options being the miso-marinated black cod. The centerpiece of the trendy Gotham Steak is a two-story, see-through pillar fortified with a selection of 500 wines. The restaurant has a bar that serves a limited menu. Reservations are recommended and can be made via the website or by phone. Open for dinner, from 6 p.m. to late.

      Gotham Steak

      4441 Collins Ave.

      Miami Beach, FL 33140

      877-326-7412

      fontainebleau.com/web/dining/gotham_steak

    Hakkasan

    • Sake and dim sum. Stir-fry and dumplings. Soups and congee. These are the offerings at the Cantonese-inspired restaurant Hakkasan, situated on the Fontainebleau's fourth-floor rooftop. The upscale, internationally recognized restaurant serves dinner every day of the week and also offers a dim sum lunch on Saturday and Sunday. The menu reads like a food haiku, with dishes such as Peking duck with Golden Osetra caviar, roasted silver cod with champagne and Chinese honey, and lotus leaf chicken with lily flower and red date. The Fontainebleau Hakkasan is one of three locations---the other two are in London and Abu Dhabi. Reservations can be made by phone or through the website.

      Hakkasan

      4441 Collins Ave.

      Miami Beach, FL 33140

      877-326-7412

      fontainebleau.com/web/dining/hakkasan

    Scarpetta

    • The AAA four-diamond-awarded Scarpetta offers Miami's version of Italian cuisine. The restaurant is elegantly casual with Atlantic or pool-side views. Scarpetta is another restaurant that has been re-created in Miami from its original New York City address. Scott Conant, founder and chef, has created a menu that stays true to its traditional Italian roots with dishes as simple as spaghetti to the more elaborate duck and foie gras ravioli. The menu includes appetizers, entrees, dessert, and a range of wines and spirits. Reservations can be made by phone or Scarpetta's website. Scarpetta is open for dinner only.

      Scarpetta

      4441 Collins Ave.

      Miami Beach, FL 33140

      877-326-7412

      fontainebleau.com/web/dining/scarpetta

    La Cote

    • Situated so as to have views of the pool and ocean, La Cote is a refined yet casual restaurant. The menu specializes in seafood, with yellowfin tuna, snapper, prawns and crab highlighting. Other options include pastas, burgers, soups and salads. La Cote's two levels offer an inspiring location to enjoy lively music, cocktails and light fare. The restaurant is open Wednesday to Sunday from 11 a.m. to 6 p.m.

      La Cote

      4441 Collins Ave.

      Miami Beach, FL 33140

      877-326-7412

      fontainebleau.com/web/dining/la_cote

    Vida

    • Vida is not your traditional American cafe. While it maintains a cafe's casual vibe, Vida surprises the palate with sushi, matzo ball soup, pizza and pasta, as well as traditional dishes like grilled cheese, tuna melts and burgers. The restaurant, set in Fontainebleau's interior, is open for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Sushi is only served with the dinner menu.

      Vida

      4441 Collins Ave.

      Miami Beach, FL 33140

      877-326-7412

      fontainebleau.com/web/dining/vida

    Fresh

    • Fresh, Fontainebleau's most casual restaurant, serves a variety of fare for dining in or takeout. The menu is diverse, featuring pizza, sandwiches, salads and gelato. As the name indicates, the restaurant uses natural ingredients to create continental food served in an informal atmosphere. Fresh is open daily from 11 a.m. to 6 p.m.

      Fresh

      4441 Collins Ave.

      Miami Beach, FL 33140

      877-326-7412

      fontainebleau.com/web/dining/fresh
