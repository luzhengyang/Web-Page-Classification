Hotel Overview & Exclusive Editorial Review

Insider Insights

  • A $19.95 per-day resort fee is charged for hotel services that include gym access, and unlimited in-room and public-area high-speed Internet access.
  • Pets are allowed with a $100 nonrefundable per-stay fee.
  • The Fontainebleau Miami Beach is a smoke-free resort. A $250 fine will be assessed to those guests smoking in their room.
Park It
Valet service: $38 per night.

The Experience

To many, the Fontainebleau is the essence of Miami-Beach style. The 1,504-room resort opened in 1954 and has maintained its role as the area's trendsetter for decades. Made famous in films like Goldfinger and Scarface, the Fontainebleau is a showcase for upscale decadence, whether youre dining, shopping or dancing. Restaurants include the amazing Gotham Steak operated by Michelin-star Chef Alfred Portale, while the on-site Liv nightclub is where the A-list goes to see and be seen. Guests will certainly enjoy the signature treatments at the Lapis spa or renting a poolside cabana to spend a relaxing afternoon sipping cocktails and soaking up the sun. And of course, the waves and white sands of Miami Beach are just steps away, in case you need to get away from it all.

Things To Do

  • Relax. Book some "me" time at the Lapis spa. Try the "Soak me" nourishing bath.
  • Play. Spend the day at the pool. If you can splurge, rent one of the private cabanas that include a variety of luxury amenities and services.  
  • Dine. Reserve a table at Scarpetta for an amazing Italian dining experience that you may not expect in Miami. Order the "duck and foie gras ravioli" or the "Mediterranean Branzino."
  • Dance. Hang out at Liv, for a night of dancing and celebrity watching. Be sure to dress smart and bring plenty of style.

What We Like

  • Some of Miami's finest dining prepared by award-winning chefs is served at the Fontainebleau. There are 12 on-site restaurants and lounges, including Gotham Steak, Hakkasan, Scarpetta, Blade, Vida, Solo and Fresh.
  • Liv a world-class nightclub with superstar DJs, celebrity clients and VIP service is an essential part of the Fontainebleau experience.
  • The Fontainebleau's aquatic complex includes multiple pools and a variety of luxury cabanas that cater to your every need. Poolside beds are also available, too.
  • Guests can groove out at BleauLive, an on-site music venue that has showcased performances by Katy Perry, Lady Gaga, Maroon 5 and Tiesto. 
  • The hotel features a collection of upscale shops that offer everything from high-end fashion collections to jewelry, shoes, watches and beach gear. 
  • The 40,000 square-foot Lapis spa features 30 private treatment rooms with a focus on thermal and mineral-rich water therapies.
  • More than 107,000 square feet of space is available for business meetings, corporate events or social gatherings.
