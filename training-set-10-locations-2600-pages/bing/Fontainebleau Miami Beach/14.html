What convention and convenience have in common in Miami Beach

The FedEx Office Print & Ship CenterSM in the Fontainebleau Miami Beach offers you virtually everything you need to meet your convention exhibiting needs — from packing and shipping to signage, copying and last-minute office supplies.

Do you need presentations, flyers, signs or other marketing materials for your meeting or event? Submit your orders online and we'll have them waiting for you at the Fontainebleau Miami Beach when you arrive.
