    Editorial review:

    This classic hotel appeared in the movie Goldfinger and recently underwent billions of dollars of renovations. In addition to that, two all-suite towers were built. There is a new 40,000-square foot spa, four nice clubs, and a steakhouse run by Alfred Portale from the Gotham Bar & Grill in New York City.

    Overview

    The Fontainebleau Miami Beach Hotel is ideally set in the heart of Miami Beach Island, close to the Muss Park, Miami Beach Bass Museum and the Miracle Mile Miami shopping center.

    This spectacular building combines Golden Era glamour and modern elegance and includes signature restaurants, state-of-the-art meeting rooms, gym, treatment rooms, swimming pool and nightclub.

    There are 1,504 guest rooms and suites spread over two original buildings and two luxury towers and fitted with HD flat screen TV, advanced sound system, oversized fully furnished balcony with magnificent views of the Atlantic Ocean and luxurious bathroom.

    Guests are offered to savor impressive Italian cuisine, Chinese dishes and regional specialties at the on-site restaurants and bars.

    The Fontainebleau Miami Beach Hotel places about 16km from the Miami International Airport and 35km from the Fort Lauderdale International Airport.

    Rooms

    Guests are provided with wide selection of exclusive rooms and suites that feature spacious bathroom with bathtub, walk-in shower, hairdryer and luxurious toiletries, large work area, king-sized bed with Egyptian cotton, plasma satellite TV, walk-in closet, private balcony, stocked mini-bar, wireless internet connection and iPod docking station.

    Dining options

    The Gotham Steak offers modern American cuisine and fresh seafood for indoor and al fresco dining, the Hakkasan provides unique atmosphere and authentic Chinese fare, while the Scarpetta is the elegant Italian restaurant with intimate ambience.

    A full breakfast is served and charges are applicable. 

    Work

    Guests are provided 107,000 square feet of indoor meeting space, including three elegant ballrooms for up to 2,128 people, two permanent boardrooms, five conference suites and flexible outdoor event space, equipped with sound systems and Wi-Fi internet access.

    Leisure

    The Lapis spa center includes 30 private treatment rooms, co-ed jetted pool and lounge area providing Lapis mineral soak, spalicious scrub, Swedish rub down massage, classic European facial, manicure, pedicure and gym facilities.

    Additional features

    The additional amenities comprise wireless internet connection in public areas, 24-hour front desk, room service, babysitting, car and bicycle rental, currency exchange, safe box at reception, airport shuttle service, laundry and dry cleaning.
