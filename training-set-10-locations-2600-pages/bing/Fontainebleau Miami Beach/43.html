Nap needed
Business
Tn
Jul 7, 2014

Great for rich couples

This is a great place if you are looking to splurge. Everything is quite pricey but the quality is fantastic. Lounge chairs were hard to come by so we splurged on a cabana and it was a great experience. Sunday night is Hip Hop night at the night club and was a bit intimidating and crowded at the front desk. The entire hotel is a 24 hour party. The night club looks like they are very choosy about who comes in- so put on your status symbols. We opted to just people watch from the bar....it was quite entertaining. Restaurant food is excellent with lots of choices for healthy eaters. The mini fridge bar doesn't allow you to add any of your own food or drink so you are pretty much stuck if you just want a small breakfast or a soda and not pay $15. If you move any of the bar snacks for more than 20 seconds your room is charged. So be careful. It was a beautiful place, great gym, excellent food and great pools. Miami and this hotel is hopping! I needed a vacation from my vacation when I got home!
