      Fontainebleau Resort Travel Tips

      Experience the Good Life at Fontainebleau Resort Vacation Rentals

      You're on vacation and it's time to indulge in a spa treatment or two. You deserve it and your body will thank you. If you're staying at Fontainebleau Resort vacation rentals, you'll have access to a variety of healthy spa treatments. You can try a facial, microdermabrasion, or a mud bath. Not only will these treatments make your stay at one of the Fontainebleau Resort condos more enjoyable, but they'll also probably improve your skin's appearance and give you a healthy-looking glow. If you're looking for stress relief, spending some time being pampered in a spa is a fantastic way to relax.

      Dine in Style While Staying At Miami Beach Beach Rentals

      After the spa, treat yourself to some fine dining at one of Miami Beach's interesting restaurants. At Ortanique on the Mile, you'll find spicy entrees like the Double-Jerked Pork Chop and the West Indian Curried Shrimp and Scallops that will leave your taste buds tingling. Or, try going to Fratelli Milano if you're in the mood for Italian. Its specialties include pear-and-cheese-stuffed fiocchi, caprese salad, and delicious tiramisu. With food this tantalizing, it's easy to overdo it; eat slowly and savor every bite, and you'll enjoy the experience even more.

      Stay at Fontainebleau Resort Vacation Rentals and Shop Like a Rock Star

      What would a trip to Miami Beach be without some upscale shopping excursions? One great place to check out is Lincoln Road, an eclectic collection of shops with fantastic people-watching, street performers, nightlife, and more. Or, venture to the Shops at Bal Harbour, where you'll encounter some of the world's most exclusive brands, such as Fendi, Giorgio Armani, and Prada. After a few hours of shopping, head back to one of the fabulous Miami Beach beach rentals to put on your new dress and get ready for a night on the town.
