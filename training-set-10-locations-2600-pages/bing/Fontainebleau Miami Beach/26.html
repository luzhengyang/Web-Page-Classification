There's a metered lot about 1/4 mile north & others across the 41st St. bridge -- about 1/2 mile. You may find some metered parking to the south on Indian Creek Drive. Bear in mind, having to walk 1/2 mile in Miami's heat & humidity in a coat & tie can make you re-think saving the parking charge.

4. Re: Public Parking near Fontainebleau
Jul 19, 2009, 6:04 PM

Just got back from a stay at the Fontainebleau, and you wouldn't believe how many times I overheard people complaining about the absurd $32/day parking rate. Guess what? There's a City of Miami parking garage just past the Eden Roc (next hotel to the north) up by 47th street, they'll charge $10 for the entire day and night! Pretty safe, and you'll be in the company of plenty of BMW's and Mercedes.

It's not In-and-Out, but who cares? You could come and go 3 times and still save money off the Fontainebleau. And what's worse, the F-Bleau's valet service on a busy weekend is horrendous (30-45 minute wait).
