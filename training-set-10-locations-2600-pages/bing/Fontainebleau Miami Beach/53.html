Glamorous oceanfront hotel with pools, nightlife

Miami Beach hot spot
Fontainebleau Miami Beach has beach access, plus shaded lounge chair and Jet Ski rentals. Once host to Elvis Presley and Frank Sinatra, the resort's see-and-be-seen reputation lives on at its LIV nightclub and Bleau Bar. Miami International Airport is a 20-minute drive.

Golf to art to meetings
The 5-star hotel is 11 minutes by car from Miami Beach Golf Club. If you're in an artsy mood, a 3-mile drive can deliver you to Bass Museum of Art. Guests here on business can reach Miami Beach Convention Center in 10 minutes by car.

Water, water everywhere
Guests rave about the hotel's multiple, luxurious pools and rental cabanas with WiFi, flat-screen TVs, and private butlers. The hotel spa combines soaks and scrubs with massages and body wraps. When you're hungry, you can choose from several restaurants and various bars.

Stylish, personal touches
Guestrooms frame surf or skyline views with huge windows or balconies, and some have separate living areas. Hotel-provided WiFi and iMacs in guestrooms let you surf with ease. Minibars, flat-screen TVs with premium channels, and premium bedding are provided to help you relax.

Special features

Spa

The two-level Lapis Spa harnesses the natural qualities of water to created a shared experience that is both restorative and renewing. Mineral-rich water in many forms - including mist, rain, and steam - are used throughout the 40,000 square foot spa that features 30 private treatment rooms as well as a co-ed jetted pool and lounge area.

Lapis draws upon thermal therapies the oldest spa treatments that transfer energy from heated elements of Earth, Water, and Air. Merging these age old techniques with the latest contemporary technology and design, Lapis provides guests with a stress-relief, anti-aging, and wellness solutions - each a sensory experience designed to energize and rejuvenate.

Spa interiors, treatments and programs are unexpectedly re-interpreted, creating an environment inspiring social engagement that is contemplative, pure, and sophisticated. A communal sanctuary, Lapis provides intimate moments within a quiet collective.
