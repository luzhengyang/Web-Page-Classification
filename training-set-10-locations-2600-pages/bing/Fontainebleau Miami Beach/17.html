    Fontainebleau Miami Beach

    from £181 per room night

     
     

    Location

    The ultimate beachside escape with white and turquoise décor, an inviting system of swimming pools and masses to do on the beach and beyond, the Fontainebleu Miami can’t be beaten for relaxed fun in the Miami sun. Offering world-class cuisine, a kids club, an award-winning spa, popular nightlife and spacious bedrooms of all sizes, this Miami bolthole has something for everyone.

    Rooms

    Blue, turquoise and white décor and furniture fills each room at Fontainebleu Miami, reflecting the colours of the Atlantic Ocean and soft sands seen from the window. With over 1000 rooms and suites split into 16 types, guests can enjoy everything from a simple guestroom to a lavish suite with a balcony, or opt for a truly indulgent two bedroom or presidential suite. All come with an iMac with internet access, an iPod dock, flat screen TV, a mini bar and modern, comfortable furnishings giving a light and airy beachside feel.

    Activities

    With multiple pools, a kids club, and long stretch of golden sand beach in front of the hotel, there is plenty of opportunity to unwind in the sun and enjoy a cooling dip at the Fontainebleu Miami. Choose a sun lounger or poolside cabana, hire a windsurfer or jetski, and enjoy the attentive service from the pool and beach staff who will ensure you’re never without a refreshing drink or snack. With the whole of Miami on your doorstep, there’s plenty of exploring, eating out, shopping and partying to be done within walking distance of the Fontainebleu.

    Dining

    The Fontainnebleu is a dining hotspot within the city, offering eight restaurants including the ever-popular Hakkasan, Gotham Steak and Italian Scarpetta. Sushi, light lunches, grills, sandwiches, ice cream and snacks are available throughout the day at five other eateries, meaning you’ll be spoilt for choice at every meal.

    Spa

    Once voted the most beautiful spa in America, Lapis Spa at the Fontainebleu Miami continues the soothing turquoise theme into a cool, calm cocoon of peace. Here the salon, gym and spa offer everything you could need to look and feel fantastic. Simply book online and pop down for your treatment, and you’ll come out floating, rejuvenated and revitalised.
