    Fontainebleau Resort condo description

    LUXURY 2 BEDROOM 3 BATHROOM Suite (ask about my junior suite as well) AVAILABLE at Fontainebleau II (Tresor Tower) Miami Beach At Discount off nightly rates charged by hotel - Tresor Two Bedroom Bay (Sunset) View Suite available for rent at the newly renovated, luxurious Fontainebleau Hotel in Miami Beach , FL. Perfect for getting away for the weekend or holidays. This Suite is offered at major discounts off the nightly rate charged by the hotel. A description of this Suite is as follows:

    Two King Beds, 3 marble bathrooms (three showers and two jacuzzi tubs), spacious sitting area with full-size sofa bed, spacious living area and dining area-fully equipped kitchen with standard-size refrigerator-oven-stove-dishwasher- microwave and washer dryer, queen sofa bed in living area, 42in LG flat screen television in living area and two 37in LG flat-screen televisions in bedrooms, entertainment system, private bar, spacious walk-in closet, two balconies providing sweeping vistas over Biscayne Bay, Downtown Miami or South Beach and one balcony with pool and partial ocean views. Kitchens also equipped with flatware- dishware and stemware. Marble bathrooms with granite counters, an oversized jetted tub and lavish shower.

    Additional amenities include high speed internet access, in-room safe and kitchenette with mini-refrigerator, sink and microwave. This room can comfortably sleep 4-8 people.

    Again, this Suite is available at deeply discounted prices off the nightly rate charged by the hotel. Please contact me via email to make further inquiries or to reserve your room(s).

    Do you need something smaller? Please contact me about renting part of this suite or my junior suite.

                              Incredible stay, great owner

                              This was our second time staying in these units. Last year we had the pleasure of dealing with Adam who responded immediately to all of our needs and made the experience unforgettable. With that as our opening we again took these units. We were 2 couples and the space was perfect. We had common space yet both couples had private space when wanted. The apartments are high end and gorgous. They are well and beautifully furnished and in impeccible condition. The apartments enjoy all of the amenities offered by the hotel, including high end bathroom products and starbucks coffee for the coffeemaker. The Fontainbleau resort is itself beautiful and the services from pool side loungers, food and drink service, to the car valets are all first rate. There is also a large gym. We love the location of the apartment as we were able to walk to south beach every morning on the boardwalk for breakfast or lunch, not needing to take out our car. At night, there are so many restos around, including some great ones in Fontainebleau itself, that a car is not really needed. We took taxis instead, so would not have to deal with parking. Adam, the owner is a jewel. He makes everything easy. He responds immediately and we never had a problem getting in touch with him during the booking process or otherwise. We look forward to staying there again next winter, and I have no hesitation recommending it to the most discerning of traveller.
