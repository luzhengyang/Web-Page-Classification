Location
The hotel is located in Miami on 8. 9 ha and rises 300 m above an Atlantic Ocean beach.
Facilities
This hotel underwent a billion-dollar renovation, is air-conditioned and comprises a total of 992 rooms spread over 36 floors. Facilities include a lobby with a 24-hour reception, a hotel safe and a lift. Parking is available for an additional fee.
Rooms
Spacious rooms and suites have 400-thread-count Egyptian-cotton linens. Each suite features a coffee machine, ironing set, a hire safe, a direct-dial telephone, a radio, a minibar, marble baths, robes and wireless Internet access (surcharge). High-definition, flat-panel TVs include premium cable channels and pay movies. High-tech extras include advanced sounds systems with i-Pod docks and iMac computers with 20-inch displays. Some rooms provide balconies and views of the Atlantic Ocean.
Sports/Entertainment
The hotel features an ocean-front pool area including a large free-form pool and a private European pool. There is also a well-equipped gym on offer to hotel guests along with tennis courts. In addition, an activity program rounds off the hotel's offerings. Luxurious private cabanas with flat-panel TVs and butler service are available for rent, as are beach cabanas and personal watercraft.
Payment
American Express, Diners Club, MasterCard and VISA are accepted.
*There is a resort fee payable by the guest*
