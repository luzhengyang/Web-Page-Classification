Fontainebleau Miami Beach Condo information


The world famous Fontainebleau Miami Beach Condominiums and Hotel is one of the more impressive structures located along the Miami Beach coastline.    Operated and managed by the world class Hilton Hotel Chain, this opulent luxury resort facilities offers all of the highest amenities and a staff of courtesy professionals that are unrivaled by other residence properties in the area.  The Fontainebleau Condos For Sale are one of the rare luxury properties that include a lavish adjoining hotel with impressive accommodations for visiting guests and family.

 

The luxuries that you would expect from such a majestic facility take place on over 1,000 linear feet of beachfront property with amenities that include such unique offerings as a water park and playground, spa services of massage, sauna and steam, state-of-the-art Fitness and Cardio Facilities,, and much, much  more.  The Fontainebleau Miami Beach even has its own 5 star restaurant and an included neighboring dance club.

 

There are over 400 individual residences contained within this luxury resort community, and no expense has been spared in the finishes and fixtures of these Fontainebleau Condos For Sale.  With custom designed kitchens of granite counters and stainless appliances, the living spaces are elegant and vibrant.  Master suites include marble flooring with interiors of pastel hues and flat screen televisions adorn every unit.

 

When you reside in the Fontainebleau Miami Beach, you are located within the most prestigious neighborhood in the most dynamic city in the world.  With the popular destination of Lincoln Road very nearby and Collins Avenue only a short distance away, residents of this stylish property can easily enjoy all of the arts, culture and entertainment that Miami Beach has to offer.  

 

You are close to La Gorce with all of its natural wonder and also centrally located to the trendy neighborhoods and hot spots of downtown Miami and Bal Harbour.  Purchasing one of the exclusive Fontainebleau Condos For Sale affords you every luxury imaginable for a dream lifestyle in Miami Beach
