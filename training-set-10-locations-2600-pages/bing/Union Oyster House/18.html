OVERVIEW / CONTACT

Located on the Freedom Trail, the Union Oyster House is the oldest restaurant in Boston and the oldest restaurant in continuous service in the U.S. — the doors have always been open to diners since 1826.

HOURS

Sunday–Thursday 11 am – 9:30 pm
Friday and Saturday 11 am – 10 pm
The Union Bar is open ‘til Midnight.

DIRECTIONS / PARKING
