“Great lobster”
Reviewed January 28, 2013

Whatever you do, you have to first ask for Sandy to be your server. She was the best and gave great recommendations for the four of us. We had the Shrimp Ravioli, Medium Size Lobster Pot, and Butterfly Shrimp. Everything was fantastic. Two of us shared the Lobster Pot-it came with a cup of New England Clam Chowder, Cornbread, Corn, Mussels, red potatoes and a 1 1/2 lb lobster. It was so much food we couldn't eat it all. Our lobster pot also included dessert a choice of Indian Pudding or Boston Cream Pie. We first chose the pudding, but Sandy suggested we sample it before we finalized our decision. It was so sweet of her to do it because we didn't like it and the Boston Cream Pie was fantastic. I loved the decor of the restaurant and the prices were reasonable. I hope to return to Boston soon and if I do I will definitely dine there again.

Visited January 2013
