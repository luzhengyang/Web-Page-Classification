DIRECTIONS AND PARKING


DRIVING DIRECTIONS
From the North (Routes 1, 95, 93)
Follow 93 South. 
When you reach the Zakim Bridge stay in the right lane and follow the signs for "Government Center" which is Exit 24A.
Take Exit 24A.   
When out of the tunnel, stay to the right toward "Faneuil Hall/Government Center." 
At the lights, turn right onto Clinton Street. 
At the next lights take a left onto North Street. 
On North Street, immediately go into the right lane. 
Take a right onto Union Street. 
We are on the right, halfway down, at 41 Union Street.   

From the South (Routes 3, 95 , 93)
Follow 93 North through the South Station Tunnel, to Exit 23 — Government Center. 
The exit ramp takes you out of the tunnel and you will see signs for Faneuil Hall. 
At the first light you come to take the left towards Faneuil Hall, and you will now be on North Street. 
Take your first right which is Union Street. 
The Union Oyster House is about 100 yards on the right.   

From the West (Massachusetts Turnpike/Route 90)
Take Route 90 (Mass. Pike) East to Exit 24B Route 93 North. 
Follow 93 North through the South Station Tunnel, to Exit 23 — Government Center. 
The exit ramp takes you out of the tunnel and you will see signs for Faneuil Hall. 
At the first light you come to take the left towards Faneuil Hall, and you will now be on North Street. 
Take your first right which is Union Street. 
The Union Oyster House is about 100 yards on the right.    

From Logan International Airport
Exit the airport and follow the signs for The Ted Williams Tunnel. 
Take Exit 24 Route 93 North. 
Follow 93 North through the South Station Tunnel, to Exit 23 — Government Center. 
The exit ramp takes you out of the tunnel and you will see signs for Faneuil Hall. 
At the first light you come to take the left towards Faneuil Hall, and you will now be on North Street. 
Take your first right which is Union Street. 
The Union Oyster House is about 100 yards on the right.

PARKING INFO
We Validate Parking for the Parcel #7 Garage, 136 Blackstone Street. If the Union Oyster House is on your right, the brick building straight ahead of you is the garage. The fee after validation is $1.00 for the first 2 hours, and $3.00 for up to 3 hours. Over the 3 hours the fee goes up to the maximum. 

PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION
Subway

Take the Blue Line to the STATE STREET stop.

Or, take the Orange or Green Line to the HAYMARKET stop.
