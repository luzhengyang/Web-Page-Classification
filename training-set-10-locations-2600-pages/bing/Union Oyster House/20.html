  • Union Oyster House

    Posted by lady at the bar on 03/15/2012

    Disappointed

    I loved it when I first went there a while ago. But when I went back for the same great foods last week, it was completely disappointing. I sat at the oyster bar, and the bartender was unfriendly, distracted that I couldn't even make simple conversation. But most of all, the oysters were so small, I couldn't believe I had to pay $14.95 for six of them. I would never go back there again.
