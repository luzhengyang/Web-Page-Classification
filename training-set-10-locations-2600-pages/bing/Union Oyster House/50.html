Our Summary Review

Billed as the nation's oldest continuously operating eatery, this 1826 Downtown Crossing "culinary museum" is a "must-go" for "out-of-towners" seeking "legit" "chowda", oysters shucked "while you watch" and other "traditional New England" seafood "done right"; the "trip back in time" "can be expensive", but you're also getting the "quaintness" of the "old-world charm" and "accommodating" service.
