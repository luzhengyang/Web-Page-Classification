Parking Office

How May We Assist You?

The Childress Klein Parking Office is located on the 28th floor of One Wells Fargo Center in Suite 2810. Parking Coordinators are available to assist One Wells Fargo Center parking patrons with registration, cancellation, billing issues and to answer any questions from 8am to 5pm, Monday-Friday. They can be reached by phone at 704-342-9030.

Please note that anyone who does not have a One Wells Fargo Center building access badge must visit the Visitor's Desk at One Wells Fargo Center to obtain an access pass prior to visiting our office. To avoid delays, it is recommended that you call the Parking Office at least 3 hrs in advance to ensure proper time for processing. We look forward to assisting you with whatever your parking needs may be!
