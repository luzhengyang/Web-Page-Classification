Bronze Bells, Hardware, Sculpture

U.S. Bells offers original designs of bronze bells, hardware, and original sculpture by Richard Fisher. From small dinner bells to large estate bells, all are hand cast at our foundry in Prospect Harbor, located in Downeast Maine. Orders may be placed using our secure online shopping cart, or you may call us toll-free: 1-866-963-7184. Our bells are also available in our store in Maine and at galleries in the U.S. and Canada.
