Welcome to the BEST WESTERN PLUS Chateau Granville Hotel & Suites

A Superior Hotel on Granville Street

Couples, families and business travelers alike marvel at the unexpected elegance of The BEST WESTERN PLUS Chateau Granville Hotel & Suites & Conference Centre, conveniently located in downtown Vancouver.

PLUS is in our name, but it's also part of our desire to give our guests more than they would ever expect from our hotel in the heart of one of the most exciting, energizing cities in the world.

Our location puts guests’ moments away from Vancouver’s best neighbourhoods, including the trendy Yaletown district and famous shopping on Robson Street. The Canada Place and Ballantyne Pier Cruise Terminals are nearby, as is a short ferry ride to Granville Island. We truly have the city of Vancouver at our doorstep.

Recent extensive renovations were executed in an effort to enhance the comfort and luxury of our accommodations. Exciting discoveries are around every corner from our 114- spacious rooms and suites to our on-site sophisticated yet casual restaurant that puts you right on The Edge of Vancouver’s eclectic and vivacious entertainment district.

We are very proud to have been awarded a prestigious 4-Key Green rating for our commitment to the environment.

Travel along with our young corporate couple (pictured throughout this website) staying at the BEST WESTERN PLUS Chateau Granville Hotel & Suites & Conference Centre as they visit the hotel for an all-day business meeting. With them, we discover the different amenities the hotel has to offer – from excellent customer service to a great selection of food  made with local fresh produce - as they go about their day.
