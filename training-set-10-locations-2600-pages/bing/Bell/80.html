    11/13/2013

    Avoid this store at all costs unless you want to be ripped off.
    I used this store when I first moved to the area since it is right up the street and therefore extremely convenient. I paid cash for a phone number change which I had been told by a Bell rep at the University would cost me around $20. Later, a $23 charge appeared on my phone bill. When I contacted Bell Service Centre regarding what I assumed was a mistake, they informed me that this Bell store was not Bell, it was a third party company that was licensed to deal Bell products. I was told to go back to the store and get a refund from them.
    When I returned to the store I talked to a sales associate who explained that I would have to return and speak to the manager about a refund which was fine. He also outright told me that "sometimes they double charge customers but other times they don't. It depends." and that in my case, there had obviously been a mistake. I took that to mean that I looked gullible but since I was now questioning it, they didn't want the hassle involved.
    I returned the next day to talk to the manager and was told that there was no way they would refund my money. I would like to add that at no point did anyone working at this store tell me that they were not actually Bell Mobility and there is only Bell signs on the store. I was also never told that there would be an extra service charge involved with this service. It may be only $20 but I am a student and that $20 goes a long way and that is besides the fact that this store admitted to ripping me off
