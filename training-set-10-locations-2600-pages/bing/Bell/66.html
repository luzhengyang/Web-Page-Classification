    Connected to Sullivan Heights Secondary School, the Bell Performing Arts Centre is most often used for local dance studio competitions and recitals, as well as high school commencement ceremonies. It is a large venue that accommodates those needs quite well, as there is plenty of seating and a large lobby to house proud parents and other family members. Sullivan Heights also uses the space for their theatre company productions, but paired with the centre's capacity for big sight and sound, performances can be underwhelming.
