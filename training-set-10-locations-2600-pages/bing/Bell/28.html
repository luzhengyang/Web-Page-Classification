In The Heart Of The Downtown Granville Entertainment District, Steps From Robson Street Shopping

Enjoy a relaxing stay at our non-smoking Ramada Vancouver Downtown hotel near Rogers Arena. Our hotel in Vancouver, BC, is the perfect place to enjoy the area. Park your car in our lot for a small fee, then enjoy all the comforts of our hotel, including free Wi-Fi Internet access and a variety of available suites.

More

LOCAL ATTRACTIONS

Head to Rogers Arena, home of the Vancouver Canucks, just five minutes from our hotel in Vancouver, BC, where you can catch hard-hitting action on the ice and concerts that you will never forget. Experience a close encounter with the playful and friendly creatures of the Vancouver Aquarium, where you can catch a glimpse of more than 70,000 aquatic animals.

Take a day trip to Whistler Blackcomb for world-class skiing in the winter and downhill mountain biking in the warmer months. If you are looking for an adventure, join Hyak River Rafting, which offers a variety of expeditions, so you can either paddle or motor your way down the rapids. BC Place Stadium and Vancouver Convention Center Complex are just five minutes from our hotel.

DINING OPTIONS

Grab a drink at the bar or sit down for lunch or dinner at Morrissey’s Irish House Restaurant, a family-friendly Irish-style bar located in our Vancouver, British Columbia, hotel. If you want to unwind with a drink at the end of the day, Ginger Sixty-Two Cocktail Den is the place to be. Two Parrots Perch & Grill is across the street from our hotel in Vancouver, BC. Whitespot is just one block away and The Elbow Room Cafe is within walking distance.
