Sad News from Evergreen Public Schools

Tragedy struck Evergreen Public Schools over the weekend when three students were killed and another seriously injured.

Two Mountain View High School students -- William McCullough and Shaun Hvass -- died in a traffic accident Friday night. In a separate incident, two Orchards Elementary students were injured while trick-or-treating, resulting in the death of 2nd grader Cadence Boyer.   

The staff at Evergreen Public Schools sends condolences to all the affected families. More information can be found on the Mountain View and Orchards websites.
