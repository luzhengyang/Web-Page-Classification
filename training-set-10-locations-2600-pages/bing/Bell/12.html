Varsity Academic Letters

Applications for CR Varsity Academic Awards are now available in the Career Center

To qualify for an Academic Letter, students must:

· be a current junior or senior at River

· have a cumulative GPA of 3.5 or higher based on at least 4 semesters of high school courses

· have participated in an ASB recognized club, sport or organization for at least one semester

· have no truancies or discipline referrals

· have an current ASB card

Applications are due in the Career Center by Friday, November 7th
