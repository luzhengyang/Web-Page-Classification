Funeral Homes in Vancouver, BC

Is there a difference between a funeral home and a mortuary? Not really, both prepare the deceased for burial. On the East coast the term funeral home is predominantly used, while on the West coast mortuary is preferred. However, mortuaries often have a crematorium on-site. When planning a funeral in Vancouver, BC, keep in mind that local funeral homes and mortuaries may differ in the cremation and funeral services they offer.
