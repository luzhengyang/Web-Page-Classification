Hotels near Canada Place Cruise Terminal – Vancouver, BC
Address: 999 Canada Place, Vancouver, BC

Use this simple form to find hotels, motels, and other lodging near the Canada Place cruise ship passenger terminal in Vancouver, British Columbia.

The Canada Place Cruise Terminal serves as one of two ports of embarkation and debarkation for the Port of Vancouver. The other is the Ballantyne.

Canada Place Terminal is located in the heart of downtown Vancouver near hotels, restaurants, shopping, and attractions. Each year between May and November, more than a million passengers sail into or out its two cruise ship terminals.

Cruise lines originating from the Port of Vancouver include Carnival, Celebrity, Holland-America, Norwegian, Princess, Radisson Seven Seas, Royal Caribbean, Silversea, and Vancouver-Alaska Cruise Lines.
