Bellingham International Airport (IATA: BLI, ICAO: KBLI, FAA LID: BLI) is located three miles (5 km) northwest of Bellingham, in Whatcom County, Washington, USA.

Due to the close proximity to Canada, the Bellingham International Airport functions somewhat as a reliever airport to the Vancouver International Airport in Canada. On the Allegiant Air website the airport is referred to as Bellingham/Vancouver.

The newly constructed gate area comprises five gates, a coffee shop and a restaurant with a bar. The second phase of a terminal expansion project will add a baggage carousel in addition to larger ticketing and pre-security areas for passengers, with completion expected by 2015.
