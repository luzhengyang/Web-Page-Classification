You can FOLLOW researchers to receive email notices when they publish or are scheduled to participate in a conference. Users following you will only receive alerts when your publications first appear in the data feed from IIS that populate this site.  Use the VIEW MY BELL LABS PUBLICATIONS option to see if any of your articles are missing, and if so, click on the link to notify the that group.
