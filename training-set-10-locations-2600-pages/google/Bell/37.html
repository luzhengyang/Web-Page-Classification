FANTASY FOOTBALL

News: Bell had 14 carries for 39 rushing yards and two catches on three targets for 22 receiving yards Sunday against the Falcons in London. (Sun Oct 26)

Spin: Bell got the start in place of the injured Reggie Bush (ankle), and while he dominated the carries, backup Theo Riddick put in a huge performance, catching eight passes on 12 targets for 74 yards and a touchdown. Bush may be able to return in Week 10, after their Week 9 bye, which could cut into Bell's touches out of the backfield, with Riddick possibly taking some of his snaps on passing plays.
