FedEx Air & Ground Players of the Week

Vote for the NFL's top air and ground performers!
One top passing performance can make a huge difference. So can one top rushing performance. And so can FedEx One RateSM -- simple, flat-rate shipping with the reliability of FedEx. Cast your vote now for the FedEx Air & Ground® NFL Players of the Week. When the winners are announced, FedEx will make a $2,000 donation in each of the winning players' names to the American Red Cross.

FEDEX AIR -- QUARTERBACK NOMINEES

Ben Roethlisberger, Pittsburgh Steelers

Roethlisberger passed for 522 yards and six touchdowns for a 150.6 passer rating in the Steelers' 51-34 win against the Indianapolis Colts.

Tom Brady, New England Patriots

Brady threw for 354 yards and five touchdowns for a 148.4 passer rating in the Patriots' 51-23 win against the Chicago Bears.

Aaron Rodgers, Green Bay Packers

Rodgers threw for 418 yards and a touchdown against the New Orleans Saints.

FEDEX GROUND -- RUNNING BACK NOMINEES

Mark Ingram, New Orleans Saints

Ingram had 24 carries for 172 yards and a touchdown in the Saints' 44-23 win against the Green Bay Packers.

Arian Foster, Houston Texans

Foster had 20 carries for 151 yards and two touchdowns in the Texans' 30-16 victory against the Tennessee Titans.

DeMarco Murray, Dallas Cowboys

Murray rushed for 141 yards, and added 80 yards receiving for 221 total yards from scrimmage, against the Washington Redskins.
