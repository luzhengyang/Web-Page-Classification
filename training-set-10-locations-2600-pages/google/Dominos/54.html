Meals

Burton Dining Hall is located in the Drysdale Student Center.

We serve hot breakfasts, lunches, and dinners daily, plus sandwiches, salads, soups, cereal, and more. You're sure to find something delicious every time you visit!

Each semester, theme meals are fun and popular events. We plan cookouts, super bowl parties, movie nights, seafood nights, and late night breakfasts during exams.

The Westover Room, Brewed Awakenings, and Domino's at Stinger's offer additional dining options. And our catering services can make any event - whether for a College office or outside group - a special one.

Guest Meals

If you have an LC meal plan, you have 2-5 guest meals (depending on the plan) you may use in the dining hall during the semester. Present your ID card to the cashier in the dining hall and request that a guest meal privilege be deducted for your visitor.

Hornet Shop in Hopwood Hall

Located in Hopwood Hall, and houses snack and drink vending machines. The machines take coins and bills.
