Greasy, but good if that's what you're into – We went to Wishbone because we live nearby and just wanted a nice Friday night dinner out after a long week of work. This is not the place for a young couple, like us, to go relax after a long work week. First, the place was full of grease and it made my eyes water and burn and my hair stink! Second, there are not a lot of options if you're not particularly into the greasy fare. If you are into the greasy fare, like my fiance, then you're in for a treat. I on the other hand found nothing appetizing. Third, no booze. I understand it is a family restaurant, it's in the title, but I was shocked to not see even a draft beer! Fourth, noisy (mainly because lots of families with young kids are there).

The service was excellent and like I said, my fiance really enjoyed his food, because he likes that kind of stuff. He had the chicken and raved about it.

So if you're not looking for a healthier meal, cocktail, and relaxation, the service, prices and chicken are great.

Good chicken, superior service – The chicken is very good. Not many places can do chicken like these folks do. But the thing that keeps me coming back is the friendly and superior service. Out of the last 7 or 8 years that I have been going I have only had poor service 3 times. The folks are friendly and very professional. In an age where service no longer has much meaning, this place rates at the top along with places like the Broker.

So if you like chicken and like being treated like royalty this is the place to go. But go early, during peak times it can take awhile to get seated.
