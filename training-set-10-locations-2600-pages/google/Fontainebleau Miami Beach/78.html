Welcome to Fontainebleau’s Miami New Years Eve Parties

If you are wondering where to party for New Year’s Eve weekend, just head down to Miami and see what all the excitement is about. The world famous Fontainebleau Miami Beach invites you to party away 2011 and welcome 2012 in high style with the beautiful people, the trend setters and the celebrity in-crowd at the most desirable destination in the country.

Fontainebleau New Year’s Eve weekend features four spectacular shows – two taking place poolside and two inside the world renowned LIV Nightclub.

Inside LIV guests will have the opportunity to ring in the New Year with 50 Cent as he performs live for the early ticket holders (9pm – 1am). Late night, the 2am tickets holders will enjoy one of the hottest DJs in the world, Sebastian Ingrosso. If you know his work, you also know that the question of where to party for New Year’s Eve has now been answered. If you want to party the night away with the in crowd, this exclusive New Year’s Eve party is the place to see and be seen.

If you are looking for a unique and truly tropical way to enjoy Miami for New Year’s Eve, head over to the Fontainebleau’s grand pool. One of the great things about hanging out in Miami for New Year’s Eve is the ability to enjoy the last night of the year without your winter parka. While others are braving the cold to watch a crystal ball drop, you will be partying with 3000 of your closest friends poolside.

The festivities get underway early with a 9 p.m. start on the last night of the year. When you arrive, you can take a seat by the pool, enjoy a drink from one of the best bars in Miami or just watch the parade of VIP guests and celebrities. When the party really gets started, you can head out to the dance floor and enjoy great music by famed DJ Axwell. Then spend the rest of the night dancing the night away at one of the most famous, and most unique, venues in all of Miami.

The party does not end on New Year’s Eve, and you can extend your celebrations into the next day with the festivities back at the Fontainebleau Pool with a special performance by Deadmeau5. The excitement gets underway at 7 p.m. on Sunday, January 1, 2012, allowing you to enjoy the first day of the new year in a big way.

Take a seat by the pool and enjoy a cool drink, hang out with your friends and watch the VIPs come and go. When you are ready for some dance action, you can enjoy the superb musical stylings of DJ Deadmau5 Unhooked. Just head to the Fontainebleau and see why it is one of Miami’s hottest spots night after night.

The Fontainebleau Hotel is one of the premier properties in Miami, and it is known as one of the best places in the entire city. This famous hotel always tops the list of the best places to stay and party in Miami, so if you are coming to Miami for New Year’s Eve this is the place you want to be.

The world famous LIV nightclub is the perfect place to start your explorations on New Year’s Eve. When you come to Miami for New Year’s Eve, you want to see the best, and that means including the LIV nightclub in your plans.

If you are coming to Miami for New Year’s Eve, you want to know that the party is in competent hands. The events at the Fontainebleau Hotel are brought to you courtesy MMG, one of the most widely respected companies in the world of entertainment. MMG specializes in bringing guests the best in nightlife and entertainment, so you can be sure that New Year’s Eve in Miami will be a night you never forget.
