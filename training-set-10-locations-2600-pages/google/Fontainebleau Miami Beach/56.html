      Event Details

      Join Modern Luxury Brides South Florida and The Caribbean and Fontainebleau Miami Beach for "Afternoon of Bridal Luxury: Something Borrowed, Something 'Bleau".

      Your day will begin on the oceanside terrace of the Glimmer Ballroom for a lingerie fashion show presented by Bloomingdale's. After the show, you'll be escorted inside for a luxuruous seated luncheon provided by the culinary masters at the Fontainebleau with a couture gown presentation by Rene Ruiz. Once lunch is complete, you'll be invited to enter the "Dream Up" showcase where four local bridal teams will come together to create four themed weddings from the dress to the decor. Vote for your favorite vignette, which will be featured in a future issue of Modern Luxury Brides! During the "Dream Up" showcase, bridal industry experts will take their turn on stage to offer tips on everything a bride needs to know for the perfect wedding experience. All attendees will leave with a luxe swag bag with products from our participating sponsors and a mini cake from the pastry team at the Fontainebleau.*

      Tickets include

      • Oceanside lingerie fashion show presented by Bloomingdale's
      • Luxurious seated luncheon with courture gown showcase by Rene Ruiz
      • Entry into "Dream Up" Showcase
      • Access to bridal experts in the "Wedding Academy"
      • Luxe swag bag
      • Individual mini cake from the Fontainebleau pastry team
      • Sponsored bar
      • Discounted valet at $12
      • One lucky attendee will win a Judith Ripka sterling silver, 18K gold and diamond Garland Cuff with a retail value of $1,950

      Agenda

      • 11:00 AM: Event opens
      • 11:45 AM: Bloomingdale's Lingerie Fashion Show with live jazz vocals from Kitty Carmichael
      • 12:00 PM: Seated White Glove Luncheon with Rene Ruiz Fashion Show and Judith Ripka giveaway
      • 1:30 PM: Dream Up Showcase Opens
      • 1:45 PM: Wedding Academy Special Guest Julien Diaz of Merge Social " Making the Memories: How to get the most out of your wedding video"
      • 2:30 PM: Wedding Academy Special Guest Susan Moynihan, Editor of Modern Luxury Brides South Florida and the Caribbean "Wedding 911: Stories of making it through the emergencies
      • 3:15 PM: Wedding Academy Special Guest Joan Love of Love. Style. Events.
      • 4:00 PM: Wedding Academy Special Guest Lourdes Sanchez of Society Cleaners "After the Wedding: What do I do with My Dress?"
      • 4:30 PM: Event ends

      For guests who want to have a full weekend experience, hotel packages are available at fontainebleau.com
