The Quality Inn and Suites Eagle Pass is located at the intersection of Highway 57 and North Veterans Boulevard. Hotel guests will find themselves minutes away from unique shopping, great dining and a variety of outdoor activities. Enjoy a free continental breakfast complete with a world-famous hot breakfast taco. Other hotel amenities include modern meeting facilities that accommodate up to 35 people and free coffee in the evening. Guests may choose a suite with a patio and sofa slapper or a poolside room with a beautiful view of the outdoor pool surrounded by palm trees.
