Fontainebleau Miami Beach

Fronting the Atlantic Ocean, this 22-acre iconic resort provides spacious guest rooms and unrivalled facilities such as 12 restaurants and bars, a spa and nightclub.

The four towers comprise some of the most stylish accommodations in South Florida. Located 1.5 miles from South Beach and Lincoln Road Mall, Fontainebleau Miami Beach is a destination in itself.

Relax in the 40,000 square foot Lapis spa, a dramatic oceanfront poolscape with a pool in the shape of a bow-tie. Dine in one of the restaurants or lounges, the three signature restaurants have all received the prestigious AAA Four Diamond award. The new fourth restaurant is called Michael Mina 74, offering an American bistro lounge by the award-winning chef.

Visit the range of shops and elegant boutiques on offer at this hotel, then enjoy a drink at one of the bars. Liv nightclub is one of Miami’s trendiest places to go and a great way to end the night.

Many guest rooms and suites have breathtaking views of the Atlantic Ocean. Equipped with advanced sound systems, 20” iMac and ultra-luxurious amenities, the sophisticated accommodations create an atmosphere of quiet and calm for effortless relaxation.
