Why Jet Luxury?

  • Jet Luxury Resorts provides hotel rates that are 20-50% below that of the hotel direct.
  • Jet Luxury Resorts is listed in the Global Distribution System (GDS) as Jet Luxury @ [brand hotel] and can be found on the GDS under the travel code WV.
  • Jet Luxury Resorts offers 10% commission to all travel agents for all of its hotels and resorts.
  • All hotel amenities are available to our guests such as the use of pools/ spas/ gyms, room service, concierge, 24-hour butler and 24-hour personal assistant service (where available).
  • The check-in and check-out process is exactly the same when going through Jet Luxury Resorts as the guest will need identification to check-in and must leave a valid credit card for incidentals.
