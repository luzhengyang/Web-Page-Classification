Profile: Fontainebleau Miami Beach

"If you create the stage setting and it is grand, everyone who enters will play their part."
   -Fontainebleau Architect, Morris Lapidus
 
A spectacular blend of Golden Era glamour and modern luxury, Fontainebleau today reinvents the original vision of legendary architect Morris Lapidus – a stage where everyone plays their unique part. The Oceanfront resort features 1,504 guestrooms and suites, including 846 rooms and suites in the original Fontainebleau buildings and 658 junior, one- and two-bedroom suites in two new all-suite towers. The 22-acre Fontainebleau Miami Beach also features signature restaurants by award-winning chefs, trend-setting nightlife venues; a two-story, 40,000-square-foot spa, expansive poolscape, a pristine Atlantic Ocean beach and state-of-the-art conference and event facilities.

THE STAGE IS SET. LIVE YOUR PART.

What inspires you? Fontainebleau Florida Hotel, LLC embraces diversity, encourages imagination and promotes collaboration. Whether your interest is sales, customer service, reservations or marketing, we offer excellent opportunities to put your unique skills and talents to work, as well as to learn, grow, create and advance.

BE WHO YOU ARE. BRING WHAT YOU HAVE. EXPRESS YOURSELF.
