Our take on Fontainebleau Miami Beach

If the name Fontainebleu Miami Beach escapes you, you've been missing some good flicks; this iconic hotel has been featured in "Goldfinger," featuring none other than the coolest of spies, James Bond. The hotel is nothing short of stunning, luxurious, and top-shelf -- from the standard guest rooms and junior suites, all the way through to the one- and two-bedroom suites, and on to the specialty suites. Only the highest quality linens and bathroom amenities will work here, so you're sure to be pleased. The location embraces the sun, sand and surf, and if you choose to never leave, you won't be bored, hungry, or thirsty. In fact, you'll walk around with a smile permanently etched on your face. Now how do you like your martini, shaken or stirred?

Insider Tips on the Best Rooms at this Hotel

Quiet Rooms

Light sleepers should request a room away from the night club, although the hotel tends to book those rooms only at full capacity.

Rooms with a View

The Standard rooms offer limited views, while the Deluxe rooms offer a view of downtown Miami or Biscayne Bay. The Oceanview rooms in the Versailles building offer a partial view of the ocean, as well as a partial view of the bay. The Chateau building offers the best ocean views.

Oversized Rooms

The corner rooms tend to have a larger terrace than the other rooms within the same category.
