Business overview

SPRING PROMO!!

Save $25 on up to half a load and $50 on half load or more!

We are a full service junk removal company!

Locally Owned and Operated and servicing all of Atlanta. Your local Atlanta operations owners are Veteran owned (USMC) and Female Business Enterprises.

Over the past 18 years, 1-800-GOT-JUNK? has grown to become The World's largest junk removal service. Started in 1989 we have grown to more than 220 franchises throughout North America. We have.. been featured in periodicals such as Forbes, The Wall Street Journal, The New York Times, Fortune magazine and Fortune Small Business and the company has been seen on shows such as the Oprah Winfrey Show, CNN and CNBC Squak Box.

About out Service:

We are dedicated to professionalism as you will see from our reviews. We have friendly, uniformed crew members who drive clean, shiny trucks. They will call you 10 - 15 minutes before they arrive and every customer will receive a follow up call with in 48 hours of the job being completed to ensure you were satisfied with our service.

We do all the loading and clean up. We go into homes and offices and carry out all the relevant items. All our customers need to do is point out the items they want taken away or label them and we do all the rest. We also do a thorough sweep up after ourselves leaving a clean and usable space when we are done.

We provide up front rates that include all weight, labor and dump charges. There are no last minute surprises when you use our service.

We have made booking easy. Our customers can book by calling our National Call Center toll free at 1-800-GOT-JUNK? (1-800-468-5865) or by booking on-line at www.1800gotjunk.com.
