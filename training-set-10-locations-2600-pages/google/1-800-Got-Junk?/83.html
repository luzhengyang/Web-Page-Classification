1-800-Got-Junk Jobs with Part-Time, Telecommuting, or Flexible Work

1-800-Got-Junk is an international junk removal company that works with homes and businesses to recycle, donate, and dispose of unwanted items large and small. Founded in 1989 and based in Vancouver, British Columbia, 1-800-Got-Junk has grown into over 200 locations in Canada, the United States, and Australia. As an employer, 1-800-Got-Junk has received awards for “Best Workplace” and “Best Company to Work For” according to BC Business Magazine. The values that guide 1-800-Got-Junk include vision, goals, growth, excitement, fun, and work-life balance. In the United States, 1-800-Got-Junk lists jobs in North Carolina, Georgia, Maryland, Florida, Colorado, and many other states. In the past, 1-800-Got-Junk has offered full-time and part-time jobs.
