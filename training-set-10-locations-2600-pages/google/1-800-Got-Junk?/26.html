    Based on my environmental background, here's what I now know:

    1 800 got junk collects trash -- for higher than their waste disposal fee to make money. For this I don't blame them. But being mislead -- nah uh. Not going to make this yelper very happy.

    They don't sort. They do what other haulers do -- they take the stuff to a transfer station nearby (not even to a municipal recycling facility). There, they get out metal, usable wood -- and all the stuff your curbside program would recycle that's valuable enough to warrant the effort, as this lowers their disposal feel.

    Don't be fooled, like I was. If you can't do the time -- sorting and donating your stuff; don't expect someone else to do it. If it sounds too good to be true, it is.
