Our Team

We're dedicated, we love our job and it shows.

Brian Scudamore

Founder & CEO

Brian Scudamore is one of our founders and is our Chief Executive Officer. He is currently the CEO of 1-800-GOT-JUNK? and WOW 1 DAY! Painting. 1-800-GOT-JUNK? has celebrated appearances on the highly-acclaimed Undercover Boss Canada, Dr. Oz, Dr. Phil and CNN. Brian lives in Vancouver with his family.

Erik Church

COO

Erik is a Founder of You Move Me and is currently its Chief Operating Officer. He joined 1-800-GOT-JUNK? in November 2011 as Chief Operating Officer. Prior to arriving at the Junktion, Erik led EF Education Canada, the largest privately-held education company in the world. Erik is a motorcycle enthusiast and currently lives in Toronto with his family.

David Garrett

Managing Director

Dave joined You Move Me as our founding Managing Director in 2012. Dave is directly responsible for all aspects of the business. Dave has a deep entrepreneur spirit, and extensive experience in building businesses from the ground up in a variety of industries including wireless, textiles and licensed products. Dave is a dual citizen of the US and Canada, and lives in Toronto with his family.

Paul Guy

Principal

Paul is a Founder and Principal of You Move Me. Paul joined 1-800-GOT-JUNK? as VP, Franchise Development working to build the systems and infrastructure for the expansion of 1-800-GOT-JUNK?. In 1999, he purchased 1-800-GOT-JUNK?'s first franchise, in Toronto, and owns franchises in New York, Saskatchewan, Melbourne and Brisbane, Australia. Paul enjoys running and travelling and lives in Toronto with his family.

Laurie Baggio

Principal

Laurie is a Founder and Principal of You Move Me. As a member of the Leadership Team of 1-800-GOT-JUNK?, Laurie helped grow the business from $4M to over $100M in revenue, expanding globally. His entrepreneurial ventures include developing products for Hasbro, Wizards of the Coast and Helikon Technologies Inc. Lauire is an avid traveller, a passionate media/technovore and lives with his family in Langley, BC.

Tom Rypma

Principal

Tom Rypma is a Founder and Principal of You Move Me. Tom has operated a 1-800-GOTJUNK? franchise in San Francisco since 2000 and has operated 1-800-GOT-JUNK? franchises in Oakland, CA and Silicon Valley. Tom currently lives in San Rafael, CA and is an avid outdoorsman and adventurer who includes hang gliding, kite boarding and snowboarding on his list of favorite activities.

Jerry Gratton

VP, People

As Vice President, People, Jerry is responsible for nurturing and evolving the award-winning culture at 1-800-GOT-JUNK?, overseeing all aspects of the employee life-cycle. He started at 1-800-GOT-JUNK? as Director, Learning & Development in 2004 and continued on as Director, People & Learning until 2009. Jerry is a husband and father of two daughters, he enjoys renovating homes and has several house makeovers to his credit.

David St. James

VP, Marketing

As VP, Marketing, David is focused on growing You Move Me's globally admired brand through strategic objectives, innovative marketing programs, and world class PR. David joined You Move Me's sister company 1-800-GOT-JUNK? in March 2006 as Sales and Marketing Manager for the Toronto franchise and through his proven track record in helping grow some of 1-800-GOT-JUNK's largest franchises and his passion for marketing, has earned his current title of VP, Marketing and Commercial sales.
