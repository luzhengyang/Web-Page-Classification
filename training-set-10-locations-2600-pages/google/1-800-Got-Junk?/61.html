Since then, it’s become a… read more booming business of professionals removing unwanted items from homes and businesses. Whether it’s an eyesore of a couch, a pile of unwanted items, or an office filled with unused furnishings, use a 1-800-GOT-JUNK? promo code and have help taking care of the heavy lifting so you don’t have to strain any muscles. Yes, that means old refrigerators, electronics, and even an alternative garbage removal service. The professional staff of trained movers at 1-800-GOT-JUNK? will show up to the location with a moving truck, assess the removal options, and provide an all-inclusive price up front—no extra gimmicks. When they remove the junk, they automatically check to see if any of the material can be recycled. And since 1989, 1-800-GOT-JUNK? has saved more than 2.5 billion pounds (and counting) of material from entering landfills. It’s as simple as a few clicks or a couple minutes on the phone.
