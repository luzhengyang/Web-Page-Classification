Description

1-800-GOT-JUNK is ready to help you regain your space! The business is a full-service junk-removal company helping residential and commercial clients  remove trash,  furniture disposal, appliances, office clean outs, construction debris, refuse, and household junk for disposal and recycling. The company was established in Massachusetts in 2003 and employs 15 people. Ms. Judy Briggs is the President of the company. All questions or problems may be directed to the attention of Ms. Briggs.

Types of Complaints Handled by BBB

BBB handles the following types of complaints between businesses and their customers so long as they are not, or have not been, litigated:

  • Advertising or Sales
  • Billing or Collection
  • Problems with Products or Services
  • Delivery
  • Guarantee or Warranty

We do not handle workplace disputes, discrimination claims or claims about the quality of health or legal services.

BBB Complaint Process

Your complaint will be forwarded to the business within two business days. The business will be asked to respond within 14 days, and if a response is not received, a second request will be made. You will be notified of the business's response when we receive it (or notified that we received no response). Complaints are usually closed within 30 business days.

What is BBB Advertising Review?

BBB promotes truth in advertising by contacting advertisers whose claims conflict with the BBB Code of Advertising. These claims come to our attention from our internal review of advertising, consumer complaints and competitor challenges. BBB asks advertisers to prove their claims, change ads to make offers more clear to consumers, and remove misleading or deceptive statements.
