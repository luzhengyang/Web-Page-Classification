Before I moved here a year ago I needed to get rid of some junk. I was tempted to use 1 800 Junk and called them for a quote. Over the phone they said it would probably cost me a few hundred dollars but they wouldn't give me a final quote until they looked at the junk. The biggest issue I had was a heavy oak entertainment unit that would fit a 32" screen TV. Now that there are very large screen TVs these entertainment units aren't very popular. On Craigslist there were many of them listed. A local company wanted a few hundred dollars to haul it away. I listed it cheap on Craigslist and got no responses. Finally, I listed it for 0$ and some graduate students responded and hauled it away.

Some of the junk I was able to donate and they pickup. You probably get postcards from organizations that take donations. I don't know about this area but in California there are places to drop off old computer equipment. The rest of my junk I took to the local dump myself.
