Web Copy Case Study: 1-800-GOT-JUNK

Get Optimized - Request a Quote

Challenge

To support its rapid global expansion, 1-800-GOT-JUNK needed search engine optimized content that would also connect with its prospects and clients.

Approach

Webcopyplus wrote keyword-rich, yet personable content for the company's main website, and also produced regional web copy for its franchise partners.

Result

The website now boasts content that attracts high levels of traffic, but also appeals to people inquiring about reliable and convenient junk hauling services. In fact, according to Alexa analytics, the site's bounce rate (where visitors immediately abandon a site) decreased by 8%.
