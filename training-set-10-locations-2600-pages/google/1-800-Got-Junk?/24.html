Business Description: 1-800-GOT-JUNK? is a professional, full-service solution to the junk removal needs of both residential and commercial customers. From a single item pick up to a whole house, our services provide cost effective, timely, efficient junk disposal. We pick up just about anything from broken appliances, worn out furniture, old mattresses, yard and garden debris, outgrown swing sets, and construction debris. You don't have to do a thing – don't crawl into the attic or basement, don’t lift anything, don’t sort anything. Don't use that dumpster rental - Call us! We show up on time at a time you schedule (even the same day), give you an up-front estimate, load the junk, and clean up. It's that easy. In an effort to keep our planet green, we donate and recycle as many items as we possibly can. In Dayton, we work with the Montgomery County Material Reuse Facility (McMrf). In Cincinnati, we have teamed up with St. Vincent De Paul in an effort to donate as many items that we can. Our estimates are free of charge and hold no obligation, but our teams are always ready to do the work! Give us a call or book online for your junk and clutter removal needs.
