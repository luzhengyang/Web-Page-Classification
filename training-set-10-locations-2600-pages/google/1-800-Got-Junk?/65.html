Green Drinks – sponsored by 1-800-GOT-JUNK?

Green Drinks | 9.30
sponsored by 1-800-GOT-JUNK?
WHEN: Tuesday 9.30 6-8PM
WHERE: The Skinny Pancake Bar Room
RSVP: HERE

Howdy Green Drinkers!
We’re super excited to celebrate our 8th year hosting Green Drinks this month!! We’re not math majors, but from our calculations that’s over 80 events sponsored by local environmentally focused businesses, and over 11,000 sponsor purchased pints of Wolavers! This month we’re pleased to feature 1-800-GOT-JUNK? We’ll be sure to do a little something extra to celebrate on Tues, Sept. 30th so stay tuned!

From our sponsor: 1-800-GOT-JUNK? is a full service junk removal company. We do all the lifting, loading, sorting, recycling, and donating for you. We even do all the clean-up! Same day service guaranteed. 1-800-GOT-JUNK? was recently recognized as one the largest recyclers, and donaters in the state of Vermont. We have also been the largest donaters of goods to Restore in the past 6 years! We run our fleet of trucks with bio-diesel. Our goal is to keep your junk OUT of Vermont’s landfill.

Thank you to our Sustaining Sponsors.
We could not do this without you!

Wolaver’s Organic Ales helps keep Green Drinks going in a big way.
For the uninformed, Wolaver’s is: “One of the nation’s original certified organic breweries, Wolaver’s is committed to producing the best beer in the most ecologically sound way.” The first 125 pints of Wolaver’s are AT NO COST TO YOU!!

Outdoor Gear Exchange
GearX.com is the online sibling of the Outdoor Gear Exchange (OGE) located on Church Street in beautiful Burlington, Vermont. Our mission is to sell great gear at great prices. We believe everyone should be able to get out and have fun! Smart buying and 16 years in the outdoor industry allows us to offer the best deals possible on a fabulous selection of gear for Camping, Hiking & Backpacking, Rock Climbing, Ice Climbing & Mountaineering, Telemark Skiing, Alpine Touring (AT Skiing) & Nordic Skiing, Kayaking, Adventure Travel & Yoga. We also have a fantastic selection of footwear and apparel from casual to technical. If you find yourself in town make sure to stop by for a visit.
Stampy Stomp 15k
Saturday Oct 4, 10am-5pm
Catamount Family Center
The Stampy Stomp 15K is an annual trail running race to kickoff Autumn and welcome in the cooler weather. Teaming up with our friends at the Catamount Outdoor Center, we are able to put on a beautiful race winding through The Catamount Outdoor Center’s trails during Vermont’s marvelous fall foliage. The day isn’t all about racing, your $30 entry fee will will get you into our post-race BBQ. We’ll have both vegetarian and meat lover options, and what better way to wind down from a race than by stuffing your face with fresh BBQ? All racers also walk away with a gift bag stuff with goodies from Mammut, Julbo, and Outdoor Gear Exchange. All proceeds from this event go right back into the Catamount Outdoor Family Center! We look forward to seeing you at the race!

Lake Champlain Regional Chamber of Commerce
The Lake Champlain Regional Chamber of Commerce is Vermont’s largest business organization and is committed to serving our 2,700 member businesses by growing the economy, advocating pro-business public policies, and providing member-exclusive innovative programs and services. The Chamber sets the pace for the future of Burlington and the Lake Champlain Region – through their rich educational programs, community engagement, and plentiful networking and business events.
Light The Night Burlington Walk
Fri Sept 26th 6-9pm
Memorial Auditorium
The Leukemia & Lymphoma Society’s Light The Night Walk funds lifesaving research and support for people battling cancer. Friends, families and co-workers form fundraising teams and these efforts culminate in an inspirational, memorable evening event. During this leisurely walk, participants carry illuminated lanterns – white for survivors, red for supporters and gold in memory of loved ones lost to cancer. You can register to walk individually or as part of a team. Every walker is encouraged to become a Champion For Cures by raising $100 or more to help support the work of hundreds of the world’s best and brightest researchers in their search for better therapies and cures for leukemia, lymphoma and myeloma. Register at http://pages.lightthenight.org/uny/Burlingt14
