About 1-800-Got-Junk?

1-800-GOT-JUNK? is pioneering an industry that is helping people remove junk, de-clutter, and live cleaner, better lives. Whether it's an old sofa or a pile of junk in the garage, 1-800-GOT-JUNK? will remove it for you. Founded in 1989, the junk removal service now has over 280 franchise locations in North America, Australia and the United Kingdom
