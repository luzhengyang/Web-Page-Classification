10BEST Says

Travel Gear Shops: Outdoor enthusiasts rejoice - Bass Pro Shop has any and all travel gear you need to make your next camping, hunting, skiing, golfing, fishing, etc. trip a huge success. The Dallas-Ft. Worth metroplex actually boasts two of these outdoorsman paradise stores - one in Garland and one in Grapevine. Choose from a wide variety of travel gear - from tents, canteens, lanterns and sleeping bags to trail and pack horse equipment. Hunters love this place, and it's no surprise, as Bass Pro Shop features all types of hunting equipment including tree stands, archery sets and equipment, optics and scopes, game calls, decoys and blinds.

Insider Tips

Local Expert Denise Brandenberg Says:

Travel Gear Shops: "Plan to spend an afternoon here - this place is HUGE!"
