Bass Pro Shops

Bass Pro Shops in Grapevine is located at
2501 Bass Pro Drive
Grapevine, TX 76051
Bass Pro Shops website

Bass Pro Shops opened its Grapevine Texas location in 1999 and has been an outdoors man’s paradise ever since. You can shop for the best outdoor gear around including these categories:

 

Fishing
Camping
Hunting
Boating
Grilling
Outdoor Wear
Guns
Knifes
Golf
And More


Bass Pro Shops is conveniently located just off of Hwy 121 in the Grapevine Mills Mall area. Bass Pro is also located just a few miles from Lake Grapevine. This is important should you need to test drive one of their many fine boats. Bass Pro Shops carries Nitro high performance bass boats, Tracker fishing boats, Sun Tracker pontoon boats, and Tahoe ski and sports boats

Bass Pro Shop in Grapevine also has one of the finest indoor gun ranges around and full-service gunsmithing.  You can also buy, sell, and trade guns in the Fine Gun Room.

You can add to your hunting and fishing skills at many of training classes offered by Bass Pro Shops. They often have events and classes for the young outdoors man as well.

With over 200,000 square feet of retail shopping space, including a huge waterfall and some of the largest aquariums in the area, Bass Pro Shops in Grapevine is a must stop attraction for anyone that loves the outdoors.
