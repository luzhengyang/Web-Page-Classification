Aquarium and Fish Feeding
Join us every Tuesday and Thursday at 6:30 p.m., as well as every Saturday at 1:30 p.m., for our live fish feedings and informational demonstrations at our 34,000 gallon, freshwater, educational aquarium. The aquarium is filled with over 400 native New England fish (courtesy of the Massachusetts Department of Fisheries and Wildlife).  Other water features include a 5,500 gallon turtle pond and a 3,800 gallon trout pool.

Nature Trail and Cranberry Bog
Come check out one of New England’s only public access Cranberry Bogs and Nature Trail. Bass Pro has teamed up with Patriot Place and Ocean Spray to offer a close look at a working cranberry bog. You can walk or snow shoe the ½ mile nature trail and checkout some of New England’s fall foliage and might even run across one of our native wild animals. You can access the trail/Bog behind Bass Pro shops.

Shooting Gallery
Kids of all ages will enjoy the logging mill themed 2-story laser arcade featuring approximately 55 animated targets.

Uncle Buck’s Snack Shop
Make sure to stop at Uncle Buck’s Snack Shop to get your fresh made fudge, roasted nuts, hot dogs, nachos, and fresh ice cream from Cumberland, Rhode Island’s Ice Cream Machine.  If you don't know where it is there’s no need to ask, just close your eyes and follow the delicious aromas.

Blue Fin Lounge
The menu features a great selection of food and beverages from tasty appetizers like Blue Fin Sliders or Three Cheese Quesadillas to our Specialty Grilled Sandwiches and Hearty Cheeseburgers.  Eat in the main room or dine outside on the patio overlooking the cranberry bog.

Fishing and Hunting Licenses - Get Yours Here!
Need a new Fishing or Hunting license to get ready for the season? Bass Pro Shops has partnered with Mass Wildlife and its new online license program and are one of the few places to get your new license. Just come into customer service and pick up your license. Special Events
Throughout the year we host a Spring Fishing Classic, Fall Hunting Classic and a Go Outdoors event. In addition we have numerous other seasonal events like Free Boat Shows, Easter Events, Family Summer Camp, Labor Event Family Fun Weekend, Professional Bull Riders Event, Halloween Bass Pro Style, Santa's Wonderland and more! Keep checking our events page for these and other events.

Mass Cruisers Cruise Nights
Come enjoy an evening with Mass Cruisers! One of the biggest cruises on the east coast is right here at Bass Pro Shops at Patriot Place. As featured on Chronicle, hundreds of cars, trucks and motorcycles cruise their way to Bass Pro to show off their pride and joy. Every other Thursday night from April through October.

Workshops, Special Courses and Store Only Events
And if all of that was not enough we also offer workshops in Fly Tying, Fly Casting and special courses like our Hunting Safety Course, Concealed Weapons Course, and USCG Auxiliary Safe Boating Class. We also have some store only events like Fishing with the Pros for Kids, and displays from local artists like Guy Harvey. So keep checking our events page for more.

Impressive indoor waterfalls, giant aquariums, wildlife mounts, delicious food and more make Bass Pro Shops an attraction for the sporting family.
