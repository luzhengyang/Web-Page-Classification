Fish Anywhere, Anytime with Bass Pro Shops latest App
 

Available Now at the App Store FREE!


Hit the lakes from your mobile device with Bass Pro Shops: The Strike Pro Angler and you could be reeling in trophy bass and competing against your friends for the biggest trophies from anywhere. Our game includes some of America’s hottest fishing spots, your favorite Bass Pro Shops fishing equipment, and a dozen of the best tournament fish species in a fun and easy-to-play format.  

Action-packed gameplay 

  • Above and below water camera views
  • Touch and accelerometer controls for precision casting, setting the hook, reeling, and intense fish fights
  • Time based Tournaments
  • Fishing Journal Quests to challenge you and earn more coins
  • Weekly Automated Tournaments coming soon!   

Useful Upgrades and Equipment

  • Earn coins by fishing, racking up high scores. 
  • Keep returning for daily coin rewards
  • Spend your coins on upgraded lures and tackle
    • Use the right tackle to target specific fish
    • Attract bigger fish with upgraded lures
    • Upgrade your line to battle the big ones  

Just Like Real Fishing

  • Two of North America’s hottest fishing lakes

  • 12 North American fish species with one legendary sized fish in each lake

    • 43 realistic lures that look and move like the real thing as well as other interesting fishing gear to help you land that trophy
