Military Discounts

Do you offer a military discount?

We have regular Military Discount Days at our Outdoor World retail stores.  Military Discount Days start the 15th of every month and last one week.  You must present a valid military identification card to receive your 10% discount at checkout.  As such, retired military personnel may also receive this discount.

Policies 

  • In accordance  with the Bass Pro Shops discount policy, this 10% discount excludes reels, electronics, firearms, ammunition, reloading equipment, scopes, bows, arrows, taxidermy, gift cards, Tracker boats, Mercury motors, ATVs, catalog sales, internet sales, restaurant food and drinks, and temporarily marked down items.  As a guideline, if an item is on sale, the military personnel will pay either the temporarily marked down price or receive their discount, whichever is greater.  Military personnel will receive a discount on permanent marked down items.
  • The 10% discount applies only to items currently in-stock and on-hand.  No rain checks or special orders.
