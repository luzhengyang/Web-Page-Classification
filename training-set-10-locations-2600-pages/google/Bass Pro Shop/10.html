Description

REAL LAKES, REAL FISH, REEL FUN!

The ONLY fishing game approved by BASS PRO SHOPS® – the #1 name in hunting and fishing!

REAL FISHING
Cast, attract, set the hook, and reel your fish in an intense fight!

LIVE TOURNAMENTS
Compete against your friends and others from around the world for the top position on the leaderboards! Use tournament winnings to buy Bass Pro Shops equipment upgrades that help you land the largest of trophy fish!

REAL LOCATIONS
Journey through North America to popular fishing locations such Lake Champlain, Castaic Lake, Lake Fork, Lake St. Clair, and Table Rock Lake!

REAL FISH AND REAL LURES
All your favorite trophy fish including Large and Smallmouth Bass, Catfish, Yellow Bass, Atlantic Salmon, Pike, Trout, and many more! Choose from over 40 official lures including worms, spinners, and crank bait to help you reel in that big catch!

TIPS FROM THE PROS
• Good casting is the key to finding trophies…aim for those boils!
• Use fish-specific lures to target Monsters
• Keep your eye on the fish meter to see if there are any interested fish nearby
• Use fish sprays and heavier line to help land those lunkers
• Come back daily for coin bonuses

GAME CENTER SUPPORT
