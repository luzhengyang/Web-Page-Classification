Manitowoc police seek info on bank robbery suspect

HTR Media 2:11 p.m. CST November 2, 2014

Police Sunday released this photo of a suspect who allegedly robbed the Manitowoc Wells Fargo Bank. (Photo: Submitted )

510 CONNECT 3 TWEETLINKEDIN 2 COMMENTEMAILMORE

MANITOWOC Police are seeking information about a suspect who allegedly robbed the Wells Fargo Bank, 3600 Calumet Ave.,at 3:30 p.m on Saturday, according to a news release from Lt. Matt Wallander of the Manitowoc Police Department.

According to the police report:

A male subject came into the bank demanding money. The subject received an undisclosed amount of cash and fled the area. Officers converged on the area, but did not immediately locate the suspect.

The suspect is described as a white male in his late 20s or early 30s, wearing a dark sweatshirt and gray winter cap. The suspect has dark blond hair, a "five-o'clock shadow" beard and was wearing rectangular glasses.

The investigation is continuing, the news release said. Police encourage anyone with information to call (920) 686-6551.

Anyone with information can also call Manitowoc County Crime Stoppers at (920) 683-4466 and be eligible for a cash reward.
