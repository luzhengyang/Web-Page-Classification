Mobile Banking Overview

Bank on the go with secure, 24/7 account access from your smartphone or tablet.

With Wells Fargo Mobile® Banking, you can conveniently pay bills, send money, deposit checks, or just check your balance—almost anytime, anywhere.

Mobile Banking starts with the Wells Fargo Mobile app. Whether you’re using a smartphone or a tablet, it’s the secure way to bank on the go. And it’s free* with a Wells Fargo checking account. With the Wells Fargo Mobile app, you can:

Send and receive money with Wells Fargo SurePaySM. Learn More >

Deposit checks with Wells Fargo Mobile Deposit. Learn More >

Wells Fargo also makes banking convenient with other features like:

  • Mobile alerts. Learn More >
  • Text Banking. Learn More >
  • 24/7 Live support. Learn More >
  • Thousands of ATMs and Banking Locations >
