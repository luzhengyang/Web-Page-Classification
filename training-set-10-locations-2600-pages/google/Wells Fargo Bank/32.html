Wells Fargo Securities is made up of nearly 4,500 team members in 40+ offices across the U.S., Europe, and Asia. We strive to create an inclusive working environment where employees of every race, gender, gender identity, and sexual orientation are valued and recognized for their unique talents and contributions. We give you the job skills and access to senior managers and mentors to help you meet your career goals.

Extensive investment banking and capital markets platform
Wells Fargo Securities' origination, sales and trading, and research teams are focused on U.S. middle market and large corporations as well as global institutional investors. We deliver a comprehensive set of investment banking and capital markets products and services to clients. These solutions range from strategic advice and distribution of debt and equity products to hedging interest rate, commodity, and equity risks. Join a team with deep, long-term relationships and the expertise to connect corporate clients with institutional and retail investors. Our associate and analyst programs primarily place applicants in Charlotte, New York, and San Francisco. Los Angeles, St. Louis, Houston, and Chicago may also have positions depending on program needs.
