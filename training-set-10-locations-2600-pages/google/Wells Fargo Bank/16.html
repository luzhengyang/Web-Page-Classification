Wells Fargo Banking On Campus

The University of Arizona is pleased to offer the University community the option to use their CatCard for ATM access and to make PIN-debit purchases when linked to a Wells Fargo checking account. Wells Fargo also offers a full-service banking location inside the Student Union Memorial Center.

The Wells Fargo banking location in the Student Union offers the newest in online banking resources. Personal bankers are on hand to show you how their system works and to assist you with your banking needs. Two ATMs are available 24 hours, 7 days a week. The branch is open from 9:00 am to 5:00 pm, Monday through Friday.

You now have the option to enhance your CatCard by adding ATM/PIN-debit card functionality for greater access and convenience. It's your ID, access card, meal card, prepaid services card, ATM/PIN-debit card - all in one.

You can use your CatCard in addition to any card you have been issued by Wells Fargo, creating the added benefit of two access cards. Please note that the usual $25.00 replacement fee will still be charged for damaged or lost CatCards.

The Wells Fargo Everyday Checking® account is specially tailored to meet the needs of college students. The Wells Fargo At WorkSM banking program for employees is available for UA faculty and staff.
