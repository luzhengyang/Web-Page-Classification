Wells Fargo Bank International

Wells Fargo Bank International (WFBI) is a public unlimited company and a wholly owned indirect subsidiary of Wells Fargo & Company. Registered and incorporated in Ireland, and based in the IFSC with branches in London and Frankfurt. Wells Fargo Bank International provides a range of banking services to bank and corporate customers in all European Union and European Economic Area countries including: deposits; corporate lending; bank lending; asset-based lending and corporate trust services. As part of the Wells Fargo international banking group, we offer a range of lending and deposit taking services in a dozen currencies.

Wells Fargo Bank International has a balance sheet of $15 billion, with in excess of 300 corporate customers and a capability to provide corporate lending in 30 EU/EEA countries and to take deposits in the majority of non-U.S. countries.
