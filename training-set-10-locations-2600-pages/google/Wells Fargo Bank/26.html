Wells Fargo Bank Branch Locations
  • County Offices

Story 1

As a convenience to residents, from November 1 through March 31, you can also pay current taxes in person at any one of the 77 Wells Fargo bank branch locations in Broward County.

  • Bank branch payments must be check only, presented with a tax bill coupon. Credit card payments are not accepted.
  • No partial tax payments can be made at Wells Fargo Banks - only full payment of the tax bill can be made.
  • There is a maximum of three transactions per person.
  • This payment option is not available for delinquent taxes.
