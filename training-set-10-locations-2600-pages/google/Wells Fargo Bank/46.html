Welcome to the website for the case of Masonek, et al. v. Wells Fargo Bank, N.A., et al., Case No. SA-CV-09-01048 DOC (RNBx) ("Masonek"). Masonek is a certified class action brought by Medical Capital investors against Defendants Wells Fargo Bank, N.A. and The Bank of New York Mellon, and one of several cases in the multidistrict litigation known as In re Medical Capital Securities Litigation, Case No. SA-10-ML-02145 DOC (RNBx).

This website serves as a portal for information about Masonek. Please review the "Frequently Asked Questions" page for answers to important questions.

UPDATE: The Settlement Administrator anticipates distributing the vast majority of the settlement funds by the end of 2013, some as early as December 23. In addition, the Court is considering an ex parte application to amend claim amounts or make name/title changes affecting approximately 400 Class Members, based on information subsequently obtained. If approved, distributions affecting those Class Members will be made shortly thereafter.
