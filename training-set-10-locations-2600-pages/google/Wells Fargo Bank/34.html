Banking

Link your University of Nevada, Reno WolfCard to a Wells Fargo College Checking® Account

University of Nevada - Reno and Wells Fargo have teamed up to offer you added banking convenience with your linked UNR WolfCard. Use it as your official student ID, for campus privileges and for your day-to-day financial needs on and off campus when it's linked to a Wells Fargo College Checking account. Enjoy free access to cash at Wells Fargo ATMs nationwide and make purchases using your Personal Identification Number (PIN) with your linked UNR WolfCard.

A Wells Fargo College Checking account offers you:

  • Monthly service fee waived when linked to your UNR WolfCard
  • Sending money to virtually anyone from your smartphone with Wells Fargo Surepay℠ service
  • Quick and secure check deposits with our mobile apps
  • Simple, secure access to your account with Wells Fargo Online® Banking
  • Direct deposit of your financial aid disbursements and/or paychecks linking to your UNR WolfCard

Linking your UNR WolfCard

  • Getting Your Card:
    • Visit the WolfCard ID Office in the Joe Crowley Student Union to have your picture taken and to pick up your card.
  • Once you have it, bring it to the Wells Fargo banking location on campus or any nearby Wells Fargo banking location, to open a Wells Fargo College Checking account:
    • Joe Crowley Student Union, 1st Floor
  • See a banker to link your card to your new or existing Wells Fargo College Checking account.
