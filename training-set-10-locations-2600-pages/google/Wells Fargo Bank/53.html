Wells Fargo – Linking Benefits

Bring in your card and link it to a Wells Fargo Everyday Checking® Account1 When your card is linked to a Wells Fargo Everyday Checking account, you can use it:

  • For free access to cash at 3 Wells Fargo ATMs on campus (2 at MMC and 1 at BBC) and at over 12,000 Wells Fargo and Wachovia ATMs nationwide.
  • At the full service Wells Fargo banking location at MMC
  • For purchases on & off campus using your Personal Identification Number (PIN)

Your Wells Fargo Everyday Checking account includes:

  • Waived monthly service fee when lined to your FIU One Card
  • Direct deposit of your financial aid and/or paycheck
  • Around the clock account access with Wells Fargo Online Banking
  • Banking on the go with Wells Fargo Mobile Banking

For more information, visit the Wells Fargo FIU One Card website.
