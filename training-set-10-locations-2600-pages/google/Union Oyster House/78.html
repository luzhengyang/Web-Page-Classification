Boston Dining - Boston, Massachusetts

A Boston Dining Guide: Links to restaurants, brew pubs and other eateries. See also: Boston Travel Guide.

Boston Restaurant Week
Boston Restaurant Week is a twice annual opportunity for Boston residents and visitors to sample prix-fixe menus at some of the city's best restaurants at a price that usually represents a significant savings. Here's a quick guide to Restaurant Week dining deals in Boston, including information on participating restaurants, upcoming Restaurant...

Whole Lotta Shuckin' Going On
From your About Guide, Boston's Union Oyster House is the oldest continuously operating restaurant in the U.S. It's a great choice for classic Yankee dining, or belly up to the famous, semi-circular oyster bar, where upwards of 3,000 oysters are shucked on a busy day.

ActiveDiner Boston
The neat thing about this guide to Boston restaurants is that if you plan your visit ahead, you can register here to receive special offers and coupons to many of the listed restaurants.

Fajitas & 'Ritas (Boston)
My brother raves about this place! Build your own fajitas, buying only the ingredients you crave--lettuce for 24 cents, for example. Here's their menu.

Legal Sea Foods
Dine in their Boston-area restaurants or use their online mail order catalog to have succulent seafood shipped to your door.

Boston Travel Guide
More resources for planning your trip to Boston and Cambridge, Massachusetts. Find and reserve lodging, design an itinerary that's perfect for you and your traveling companions and discover all of the treats the city is known for, plus a few less-well-known Boston haunts, too.

Boston Best Breakfast Sandwich - Mike & Patty's
Looking for the best breakfast sandwich in Boston? You'll find it at Mike & Patty's, a tiny restaurant hidden away in a Boston neighborhood you've probably never heard of.
