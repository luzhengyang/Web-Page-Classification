Union Oyster House

  • 41 Union Street
  • Boston, MA 02108
  • Neighborhood: Boston - Downtown/Financial District
  • 617-227-2750
  • Fax: 617-227-2306
  • Visit Website
  • Details
  • Amenities

America's oldest restaurant, established in 1826, is located on the Freedom Trail near Faneuil Hall. Specializing in hearty portions of Yankee-style seafood, lobster and shore dinners, served in a lively, quaint historic setting. Children's menu, full bar, private dining room, reservations recommended.
