Union Oyster House

Union Oyster House
Oil
20 x 20
Sold

Union Oyster House is the oldest restaurant in Boston. They have a classic semi-circular oyster bar where I had a dozen raws and a Bloody Mary...hair of the dog after a fun night out on the town and a great way to start a day in Faneuil Hall. A "must" if you find yourself in the area.
